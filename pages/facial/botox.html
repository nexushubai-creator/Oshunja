Overview

Botox is one of the most popular non-surgical aesthetic treatments worldwide, and at OSHUNJA in Kingston, Jamaica, we specialize in delivering natural-looking results that enhance your beauty without altering your unique expressions. Botox works by temporarily relaxing the muscles responsible for dynamic wrinkles‚Äîthose lines that appear when you smile, frown, or squint.

The active ingredient, botulinum toxin type A, blocks nerve signals to targeted facial muscles, allowing them to relax. This smooths existing wrinkles and prevents new ones from forming with continued use. Our experienced practitioners understand facial anatomy and use precise injection techniques to ensure balanced, harmonious results that look completely natural.

Whether you're noticing your first fine lines or seeking to maintain a youthful appearance, Botox offers a quick, effective solution with minimal downtime. It's ideal for professionals, parents, and anyone in Kingston looking to refresh their appearance without surgery or lengthy recovery periods.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does Botox last?

Botox results typically last 3-4 months. Some patients may experience results lasting up to 6 months with repeated treatments as muscles become trained to relax. Individual metabolism, muscle strength, and treatment area can influence duration.

Is Botox painful?

Most patients describe Botox injections as a minor pinch or slight pressure. The needles used are very fine, and the procedure takes only 10-15 minutes. Ice or numbing cream can be applied for sensitive patients, though most find it unnecessary.

When will I see Botox results?

Initial results appear within 3-5 days, with full effects visible after 10-14 days. This gradual onset creates natural-looking improvement that won't have people wondering what you "had done."

What areas can be treated with Botox?

Common treatment areas include forehead lines, frown lines (between eyebrows), crow's feet around eyes, bunny lines on the nose, lip lines, chin dimpling, and neck bands. Botox also effectively treats excessive underarm sweating (hyperhidrosis), jaw clenching (TMJ), and chronic migraines.

Can Botox look natural?

Absolutely! When administered by skilled practitioners at proper doses, Botox smooths wrinkles while maintaining natural facial expressions. The goal at OSHUNJA is a refreshed, not frozen, appearance. You'll still be able to show emotion and expression naturally.

What are the possible side effects?

Common, temporary side effects include mild bruising, redness, or swelling at injection sites. Rarely, patients may experience headache or temporary drooping if Botox migrates (this resolves within weeks). Serious complications are extremely rare when performed by qualified practitioners.

How much does Botox cost in Kingston, Jamaica?

Botox is typically priced per unit or per treatment area. Cost varies based on the number of areas treated and amount of product needed. During your consultation, we'll provide a personalized quote based on your specific goals.

Is Botox safe for dark skin?

Yes! Botox works on muscles, not skin pigmentation, making it safe and effective for all skin tones including melanin-rich Caribbean skin. Our physicians have extensive experience treating diverse patients in Kingston.

Can I get Botox if I'm planning pregnancy?

We do not recommend Botox during pregnancy or breastfeeding as a precaution. If you're planning to become pregnant, discuss timing with your physician during consultation.

What age should I start Botox?

Many patients start "preventative Botox" in their mid-to-late 20s or early 30s to prevent deep wrinkles from forming. Others wait until lines are more visible. The ideal age depends on your skin, genetics, and personal goals.
