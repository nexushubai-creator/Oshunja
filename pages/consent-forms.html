Informed Consent

Before any procedure, you will be required to sign informed consent forms that explain:

  • The nature of the procedure
  • Expected outcomes and benefits
  • Potential risks and complications
  • Alternative treatment options
  • Recovery expectations
  • Post-procedure care requirements

Photography Consent

For medical documentation and before/after comparisons, we may request permission to take photographs. These images are stored securely and used only with your explicit consent.
