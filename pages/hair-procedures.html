Hair Restoration & Treatment

Restore your confidence with advanced hair restoration in Kingston, Jamaica

PRP Hair Restoration

Platelet-rich plasma injections stimulate hair follicles and promote natural hair growth. Non-surgical treatment using your own growth factors with minimal downtime.

Learn More

Hair Transplant (FUE/FUT)

Surgical hair restoration using advanced follicular techniques. Permanent solution for pattern baldness with natural-looking results using your own hair.

Explore Transplants

Medical Hair Loss Treatment

Prescription medications including finasteride and minoxidil to slow hair loss and promote regrowth. Evidence-based pharmaceutical approach with ongoing medical supervision.

View Treatments

Low-Level Laser Therapy (LLLT)

FDA-cleared laser therapy stimulates hair follicles and improves hair density. Non-invasive treatment for thinning hair with painless sessions and cumulative results.

Scalp Microneedling

Microneedling stimulates scalp circulation and collagen production to support hair growth. Often combined with topical growth factors or PRP for enhanced results.
