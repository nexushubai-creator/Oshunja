Overview

Botox at OSHUNJA is a physician-performed neuromodulator treatment designed to relax the dynamic muscles that create forehead lines, frown lines, and crow's feet. Your session begins with digital facial mapping so we can study how you animate, then craft a dosing plan that preserves natural expression while refining key zones.

Kingston's tropical climate, sun exposure, and vibrant social calendar demand results that look effortless in every setting. We balance medical precision with an aesthetic point of view, layering skincare coaching and medical-grade aftercare so your complexion remains smooth long after the appointment.

Every consultation includes a discussion about your wellness routines and personal milestones, ensuring your maintenance calendar fits seamlessly into your lifestyle.

How It Works & What To Expect

1

Private Consultation

Meet with our physician to discuss your concerns and desired outcomes. We'll perform facial photography and muscle assessment to guide precise placement.

2

Pre-Treatment Preparation

Skin is cleansed and prepped. Topical numbing cream is available but rarely required thanks to ultra-fine needles.

3

The Procedure

OnabotulinumtoxinA is administered via ultra-fine needles in micro-droplets across treatment zones. Most patients describe quick pinches.

4

Immediate Aftercare

Targeted pressure and soothing serum are applied. You depart with personalized lifestyle notes and follow-up scheduling.

Ideal Candidates & Considerations

You May Be an Ideal Candidate If:

  • You have dynamic wrinkles from facial expressions
  • You're seeking preventative or corrective wrinkle care
  • You want natural-looking refinement
  • You're in good overall health
  • You have realistic expectations about results
  • You're looking for a non-surgical solution

Important Considerations:

  • Pregnancy/Breastfeeding: Not advised during these periods
  • Medical Conditions: Disclose neuromuscular disorders
  • Medications: Report anticoagulants and supplements
  • Timing: Schedule 2+ weeks before major events
  • Skin Type: Safe for all skin tones including melanated skin
Contraindications: This procedure is not recommended for those with active neuromuscular disorders, allergies to botulinum toxin, or infections at injection sites. A thorough medical evaluation will determine your candidacy.

Recovery & Aftercare

First 4 Hours

  • Stay upright to allow product to settle evenly
  • Avoid rubbing or massaging treated areas
  • No lying down or bending over
  • Resume normal activities

First 24 Hours

  • Skip intense workouts and hot environments
  • Avoid saunas, hot yoga, and pool activities
  • Use SPF 50+ sunscreen before sun exposure
  • Minor redness resolves within 30 minutes

First Week

  • Results begin to appear within 3-5 days
  • Resume all normal activities
  • Stay hydrated to support results
  • Avoid facial treatments until settled

2 Weeks+

  • Peak results fully visible
  • Optional refinement visit if needed
  • All activities resumed
  • Schedule next maintenance appointment

Aftercare Instructions

Position: Stay upright for 4 hours post-treatment
Activity: Avoid intense exercise for 24 hours
Sun Protection: Use SPF 50+ when outdoors
Pressure: Don't rub or massage treated areas
Hydration: Drink plenty of water to support results
Follow-up: Schedule refinement visit at 2 weeks if needed
When to Contact Us: Call immediately if you experience severe headache, vision changes, difficulty swallowing, or asymmetric results beyond 48 hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

How soon will I see results?

Softening starts within 3-5 days with peak refinement visible at the 2-week mark. Results develop gradually for a natural appearance.

Will I look frozen?

No. Micro-dosing and facial mapping keep expression natural while easing targeted lines. Our goal is refreshed, not frozen.

How frequently should I schedule treatments?

Most Kingston clients return every 3-4 months. Consistency trains muscles to relax longer over time, potentially extending intervals.

Can Botox be paired with fillers?

Yes. Many patients combine neuromodulators with Juvederm in one visit for balanced rejuvenation addressing both lines and volume loss.

Is it safe for darker skin tones?

Absolutely. Botox acts on muscle receptors and is pigment-agnostic when delivered by trained physicians. Safe for all skin types.

Does it hurt?

Most patients describe quick pinches. Ultra-fine needles minimize discomfort. Topical numbing is available but rarely needed.

When can I exercise after treatment?

Wait 24 hours before intense workouts to allow the product to settle properly. Light walking is fine immediately after.

How much does Botox cost in Jamaica?

Cost varies based on treatment areas and units needed. During your consultation, we'll provide a detailed quote. We offer flexible payment plans.

Can men get Botox?

Absolutely. We treat many male patients seeking natural wrinkle reduction. Dosing is adjusted for masculine facial structure.

What if I'm not satisfied with results?

We offer complimentary refinement visits at 2 weeks. Botox is temporary, so any adjustments can be made at your next appointment.

Ready to Smooth and Refresh?

Schedule a consultation with our aesthetic physicians to discuss your Botox treatment. We'll create a personalized plan that preserves your natural expressions while softening dynamic wrinkles.
