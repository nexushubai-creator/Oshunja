Overview

Microneedling creates micro-channels in the skin to awaken collagen production and accelerate cellular turnover. Our physicians tailor needle depth for Caribbean skin tones, addressing acne scars, enlarged pores, and uneven texture without compromising pigment.

Each session pairs the latest device technology with stem-cell conditioned media or platelet-rich plasma, depending on your concerns. Cooling masks, red-light therapy, and medical skincare ensure your post-treatment skin is soothed and luminous.

Because Kingston's humidity and sunshine can stress the skin barrier, we design maintenance plans that include sunscreen, antioxidants, and hydration rituals that fit your lifestyle.

How It Works & What To Expect

1

Consultation & Skin Analysis

Face is cleansed, analyzed under LED light, and assessed for treatment depth. We discuss your skin concerns and create a customized treatment plan.

2

Numbing & Preparation

Topical numbing cream applied for 20 minutes to ensure comfort during treatment. Skin is prepped with antiseptic solution.

3

The Microneedling Process

Medical-grade device creates controlled micro-injuries at customized depths (0.5-2.5mm). Regenerative serums or PRP infused during treatment for enhanced results.

4

Post-Treatment Care

LED light or cooling masque calms the skin. You leave with curated post-care kit including gentle cleanser, recovery serum, and SPF.

Ideal Candidates & Considerations

You May Be an Ideal Candidate If:

  • You have acne scars or textural irregularities
  • You want to minimize enlarged pores
  • You seek skin rejuvenation with minimal downtime
  • You're in good overall health
  • You have realistic expectations
  • You're committed to proper aftercare

Important Considerations:

  • Active Acne: Should be treated before microneedling
  • Retinoids: Discontinue 5 days prior to treatment
  • Sun Exposure: Avoid before and after treatment
  • Pregnancy: Consult physician before booking
  • Skin Conditions: Active eczema or rosacea may require delay
Contraindications: Not recommended for active infections, cold sores, keloid scarring tendency, or certain autoimmune conditions. Consultation will determine candidacy.

Recovery & Aftercare

First 24 Hours

  • Expect sun-kissed flush and mild redness
  • Avoid makeup until the next day
  • Cleanse with lukewarm water only
  • Apply provided recovery serum

Days 2-3

  • Redness fades significantly
  • Use gentle cleanser provided in kit
  • Apply mineral SPF 50 before going outdoors
  • Avoid intense exercise and heat

Days 3-5

  • Temporary flaking may occur
  • Resume normal activities
  • Avoid pools, gyms, and saunas
  • Keep skin hydrated and protected

Long-Term Care

  • Resume active skincare after 5 days
  • Schedule next session in 4 weeks
  • Maintain daily SPF protection
  • Follow prescribed home care routine

Aftercare Instructions

Cleansing: Lukewarm water and gentle cleanser only
Sun Protection: SPF 50+ and reapply every 2 hours
Hydration: Use provided recovery serum twice daily
Activity: Avoid pools and gyms for 5 days
Products: Skip active ingredients for 5 days
Makeup: Wait 24 hours before applying
When to Contact Us: Call if you experience severe pain, signs of infection, or unusual reactions beyond normal redness.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many sessions will I need?

Most clients begin with a series of 3 treatments spaced 4 weeks apart, then shift to quarterly maintenance. Deeper concerns like scarring may require 6 sessions.

Is there downtime?

Expect mild redness for up to 48 hours. Makeup can be applied the next day if needed. Most people return to work immediately.

Does microneedling hurt?

Topical numbing cream makes treatment comfortable. Most describe it as tolerable vibration or scratching sensation.

Can microneedling be combined with PRP?

Yes! Pairing with platelet-rich plasma amplifies collagen stimulation and glow for enhanced results.

Is it safe for dark skin?

Absolutely. Our physicians customize depth and technique for Caribbean skin tones to avoid hyperpigmentation and achieve optimal results.

When will I see results?

Notice fresher texture within one week with cumulative improvement after each session in your series.

Can I do this before an event?

Yes, but plan for at least 3-5 days before the event to allow redness to subside. Optimal results appear 4-6 weeks after treatment.

How much does microneedling cost in Jamaica?

Cost varies based on treatment areas and whether PRP is added. During consultation, we'll provide detailed pricing and package options.

What's the difference between microneedling and dermarolling?

Medical microneedling uses precision-controlled devices with adjustable depths. Dermarolling at home is less effective and carries higher infection risk.

Can I use retinol after treatment?

Wait 5-7 days before resuming active ingredients like retinol to allow skin barrier to heal fully.
