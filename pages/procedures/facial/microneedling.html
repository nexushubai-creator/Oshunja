Overview

Microneedling at OSHUNJA uses medical-grade devices to create controlled micro-injuries that trigger your skin's natural healing response. This stimulates collagen and elastin production, improving skin texture, reducing acne scars, minimizing pores, and diminishing fine lines—all with minimal downtime.

Kingston's tropical climate can be challenging for skin health. Our advanced microneedling treatments are customized for Caribbean skin tones, addressing concerns like hyperpigmentation, sun damage, and uneven texture with precision and safety.

We often combine microneedling with platelet-rich plasma (PRP) from your own blood for enhanced results. This "vampire facial" approach accelerates healing and amplifies collagen production for superior skin rejuvenation.

How It Works & What To Expect

1

Skin Assessment & Consultation

We evaluate your skin concerns—acne scars, texture, pores, fine lines—and determine the appropriate needle depth and treatment plan for your unique skin type and goals.

2

Preparation & Numbing

Your face is thoroughly cleansed and a topical numbing cream is applied for 20-30 minutes to ensure comfort throughout the treatment.

3

Microneedling Treatment

A medical-grade microneedling device creates thousands of controlled micro-channels in the skin. We adjust depth based on treatment area—deeper for scars and texture, lighter for sensitive zones. Optional serums or PRP can be applied simultaneously for enhanced results.

4

Post-Treatment Care

Soothing serums and growth factors are applied. You'll receive detailed aftercare instructions and leave with some redness that typically fades within 24-48 hours.

5

Series & Follow‑Up

We typically recommend 3–6 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart. We'll reassess progress and plan maintenance for lasting benefits.

Ideal Candidates & Considerations

Microneedling May Be Right for You If:

  • You have acne scars—especially rolling, boxcar, or shallow ice-pick scars—that you want to soften and improve
  • Your skin texture is rough, uneven, or has enlarged pores that make makeup application difficult
  • You're seeing early signs of aging like fine lines and want to boost collagen naturally without injectables
  • You have hyperpigmentation, sun damage, or melasma that topical products haven't fully resolved
  • You want to enhance the effectiveness of your medical-grade skincare products
  • You're willing to undergo a series of 3-6 treatments spaced 4-6 weeks apart for best results
  • You can avoid sun exposure and commit to strict sun protection during treatment series

Important Safety Considerations:

  • Active Acne: If you have active, inflamed acne breakouts, we'll need to treat those first before microneedling—treating over active lesions can spread bacteria
  • Skin Conditions: Active rosacea, eczema, psoriasis, or infections at treatment sites require postponement until skin is calm and clear
  • Keloid Tendency: If you form keloids or thick scars easily, microneedling carries increased risk and may not be appropriate
  • Sun Exposure: Must avoid direct sun for 2 weeks before and after—challenging in Jamaica but critical for safety and results
  • Medications: Blood thinners, retinoids, and certain acne medications may need adjustment before treatment—discuss all medications during consultation
  • Realistic Expectations: Results are gradual over months, not instant. Deep scars improve but rarely disappear completely—we'll show you realistic before/after examples
Contraindications: Microneedling is not recommended for those with active infections, open wounds, active acne, keloid scarring tendency, blood clotting disorders, or immunosuppression. A thorough skin assessment will determine candidacy.

Recovery & Aftercare

First 24 Hours

  • Expect moderate redness and slight swelling
  • Skin may feel warm, tight, or sensitive to touch
  • Avoid touching or rubbing your face
  • Apply only approved post-treatment serums
  • Sleep with head elevated to reduce swelling

Days 2-4

  • Redness significantly diminishes
  • Light peeling or flaking may occur (normal!)
  • Don't pick at peeling skin—let it shed naturally
  • Resume gentle cleansing with approved products
  • Most patients return to work by day 3-4

First Week

  • All visible redness resolved
  • Skin begins to look brighter and smoother
  • Resume light makeup (mineral-based preferred)
  • Continue strict sun protection
  • Avoid retinoids and harsh actives for 5-7 days

Weeks 2-6

  • Collagen production begins accelerating
  • Gradual improvement in texture and tone
  • Resume normal skincare routine after one week
  • Schedule next treatment in series (4-6 weeks apart)

Aftercare Instructions

Sun Protection: Critical! Use SPF 50+ daily and avoid direct sun in Jamaica's tropical climate
Gentle Cleansing: Use lukewarm water and gentle, fragrance-free cleansers for first week
Hydration: Keep skin moisturized with approved, gentle, non-comedogenic products
Avoid Actives: No retinoids, acids, or exfoliants for 5-7 days post-treatment
Exercise: Avoid intense workouts for 24-48 hours to prevent excessive sweating
Heat Exposure: No saunas, hot tubs, or steam for one week
When to Contact Us: Call if you experience severe pain, signs of infection (increasing warmth, pus, fever), prolonged swelling, or unusual reactions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can I travel after liposuction 360?

Plan to remain in Kingston for at least seven days for follow-ups and lymphatic care. This ensures optimal healing and allows our team to monitor your progress during the critical early recovery period.

When can I return to work?

Desk work usually resumes in 7 to 10 days; more active roles may need three weeks. We provide work notes as needed and will guide you based on your specific job requirements and healing progress.

Do I have to wear compression?

Yes. Continuous compression is vital for shaping and controlling swelling for six weeks. The compression garment supports your new contours and significantly improves your final results.

Will fat come back?

Removed fat cells do not return, but remaining cells can grow if weight increases, so maintenance is key. With a stable weight and healthy lifestyle, your results will be long-lasting.

How much fat can be removed safely?

Safety guidelines typically limit removal to 5 liters or about 5% of body weight. Our surgeons prioritize your safety and will determine the appropriate amount based on your anatomy and health status.

Is liposuction 360 painful?

Most patients describe moderate discomfort rather than pain, easily managed with prescribed medication. The tumescent technique used helps minimize post-operative discomfort.

Can this replace diet and exercise?

No, this is not a weight-loss procedure. It's designed for body contouring and removing stubborn fat pockets that resist diet and exercise. Best results come when you're near your goal weight.

What about scarring?

Incisions are tiny (3-5mm) and strategically placed to be inconspicuous. With proper care, they fade to barely visible marks within 6-12 months.

How much does liposuction 360 cost in Jamaica?

The cost varies depending on the extent of treatment and whether it's combined with other procedures. During your consultation, we'll provide a detailed quote. We offer flexible payment plans to make treatment accessible.

Is the procedure safe for dark skin?

Absolutely. Our surgeons have extensive experience treating patients of all skin tones, including melanin-rich Caribbean skin. We use techniques specifically tailored to optimize healing for darker skin types.
