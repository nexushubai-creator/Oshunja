Overview

Juvederm at OSHUNJA uses FDA-approved hyaluronic acid fillers to restore lost facial volume, smooth static wrinkles, and enhance your natural contours. Our Kingston-based aesthetic physicians combine medical expertise with an artistic eye to deliver results that look refreshed, never overdone.

Kingston's tropical climate and vibrant lifestyle demand results that maintain their beauty in every setting. We use advanced injection techniques and premium products specifically chosen for their longevity and natural appearance in warm, humid conditions.

Each Juvederm treatment plan is customized to your unique facial anatomy and aesthetic goals—whether you're addressing nasolabial folds, enhancing lips, restoring cheek volume, or refining your jawline. Our team ensures you achieve balanced, beautiful results that enhance your confidence.

How It Works & What To Expect

1

Personalized Consultation

Meet with our aesthetic injector to discuss your goals and examine your facial structure. We'll create a customized treatment plan identifying which areas will benefit most from volume restoration and how to achieve natural-looking balance.

2

Treatment Preparation

Your skin is gently cleansed and treatment areas are marked. We apply topical numbing cream for comfort, though Juvederm contains built-in lidocaine that provides additional pain relief during injection.

3

Precise Injection

Using ultra-fine needles or blunt-tip cannulas, our injector precisely places hyaluronic acid filler beneath your skin. We carefully sculpt and massage the product to ensure optimal placement, natural contours, and immediate visible results.

4

Immediate Results & Aftercare

You'll see instant volume restoration, though mild swelling may mask final results for 1-2 weeks. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and schedule a complimentary follow-up appointment for assessment and any desired touch-ups.

5

2‑Week Review & Optimization

We reassess at two weeks once swelling has settled. Minor touch‑ups can be performed to fine‑tune symmetry and ensure beautifully natural results.

Ideal Candidates & Considerations

You May Be an Ideal Candidate If:

  • You want to restore lost facial volume or enhance your features naturally
  • You have static wrinkles, nasolabial folds, or areas of volume loss
  • You're seeking immediate, visible results without surgery
  • You want to enhance lips, cheeks, or refine facial contours
  • You're in good health with realistic expectations about results
  • You're not pregnant, breastfeeding, or have active facial infections
  • You want a reversible treatment option with minimal downtime

Important Considerations:

  • Pregnancy: Not recommended during pregnancy or breastfeeding; wait until after
  • Active Infections: Cold sores or skin infections must be treated before injection
  • Allergies: Inform us of allergies to lidocaine or hyaluronic acid products
  • Blood Thinners: May increase bruising risk; discuss medications with injector
  • Realistic Expectations: Fillers enhance features but don't completely transform appearance
  • Maintenance: Results last 9-18 months; periodic touch-ups needed to maintain results
Contraindications: Juvederm is not recommended for those with severe allergies to lidocaine, previous allergic reactions to hyaluronic acid fillers, active skin infections, or history of keloid scarring at injection sites.

Recovery & Aftercare

First 24 Hours

  • Mild swelling and redness at injection sites are normal and expected
  • Apply ice packs for 10-15 minutes each hour to reduce swelling
  • Sleep elevated to minimize swelling overnight
  • Avoid strenuous exercise, alcohol, and excessive heat
  • Don't massage treated areas unless specifically instructed

First Week

  • Most swelling resolves within 3-5 days
  • Avoid intense heat sources (saunas, hot yoga, direct sun)
  • Gentle cleansing and normal skincare okay after 24 hours
  • Resume all normal daily activities immediately
  • Avoid dental work for 2 weeks if lips were treated

2 Weeks

  • Final results visible as any residual swelling subsides
  • Filler has fully integrated with your facial tissues
  • Resume all normal activities including facials and treatments
  • Schedule follow-up if touch-ups or adjustments desired

Long-Term (9-18 Months)

  • Results last 9-18 months depending on product used
  • Maintain results with periodic touch-ups as filler metabolizes
  • Protect skin with daily SPF to preserve results
  • Stay well-hydrated to support hyaluronic acid longevity

Aftercare Instructions

Ice Application: Ice packs and head elevation for first 24-48 hours minimize swelling
Activity Limits: Avoid strenuous exercise for 24 hours; resume normal activities after
Heat Avoidance: Skip saunas, hot tubs, and intense sun exposure for one week
Gentle Care: Light cleansing okay; avoid aggressive facial massage for one week
Makeup: Can apply makeup 24 hours after treatment if no complications
Follow-up: Return in 2 weeks for complimentary assessment and any adjustments
When to Contact Us: Call immediately if you experience severe pain, skin blanching (white patches), vision changes, difficulty breathing, or signs of vascular compromise.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Juvederm hurt?

Most patients describe only mild pressure and brief pinching sensations. Topical numbing cream and the built-in lidocaine in Juvederm minimize discomfort. Many say it's much easier than expected.

How long does Juvederm last?

Typically 9-18 months depending on the specific product used, treatment area, and your individual metabolism. Lips tend to metabolize faster (9-12 months) while cheeks last longer (12-18 months).

Will I look swollen or unnatural?

Mild swelling for 1-2 weeks is normal, but most patients can continue their daily activities. Our conservative approach ensures results look refreshed and natural, never overdone or "pillow-faced."

Can Juvederm be reversed?

Yes! Hyaluronic acid fillers can be quickly dissolved with hyaluronidase enzyme if you're unhappy with results or need adjustments. This safety feature sets Juvederm apart from permanent fillers.

When will I see final results?

Results are visible immediately, though mild swelling may mask the final outcome for 1-2 weeks. Once swelling resolves, you'll see your beautiful, natural-looking results that last 9-18 months.

How much does Juvederm cost in Kingston?

Pricing varies by treatment area and amount of product needed. During your consultation, we provide detailed, transparent quotes. We also offer package pricing for multiple areas or maintenance plans.

Can I combine Juvederm with Botox?

Absolutely! Botox relaxes dynamic wrinkles (expression lines) while fillers restore volume and smooth static wrinkles. They complement each other beautifully for comprehensive facial rejuvenation.

When can I wear makeup after Juvederm?

Wait 24 hours, then normal makeup application is fine. Just avoid aggressive rubbing or pressure on treated areas for the first week to allow filler to settle properly.

Is Juvederm safe for darker skin tones?

Yes! Juvederm works the same for all skin tones. Our Kingston injectors have extensive experience treating Caribbean skin and understand the unique considerations for melanin-rich complexions.

What if I don't like my results?

Hyaluronic acid fillers can be dissolved with hyaluronidase if needed. We also offer complimentary 2-week follow-ups for minor adjustments. Your satisfaction is our priority.
