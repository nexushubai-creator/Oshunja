How It Works & What To Expect

1

Consultation & Analysis

Comprehensive facial assessment to identify areas for volume restoration, contouring, or enhancement. We discuss your goals and create a customized injection plan.

2

Preparation

Treatment areas are cleansed and marked. Topical numbing cream applied for comfort, though Juvederm contains built-in lidocaine for additional pain relief.

Using fine cannulas or needles, filler is precisely placed in targeted areas. Treatment takes 30-60 minutes depending on areas treated.

Gentle massage ensures smooth distribution. You depart with aftercare instructions and can resume most normal activities immediately.

Ideal Candidates & Considerations

You May Be an Ideal Candidate If:

  • You have volume loss in cheeks, temples, or lips
  • You want to enhance facial contours naturally
  • You seek non-surgical facial rejuvenation
  • You're in good overall health
  • You have realistic expectations about results
  • You're age 21 or older

Important Considerations:

  • Pregnancy: Not recommended during pregnancy or breastfeeding
  • Allergies: Discuss any allergies to lidocaine or hyaluronic acid
  • Medical Conditions: Bleeding disorders require special precautions
  • Medications: Avoid blood thinners before treatment
  • Timing: Schedule 2+ weeks before major events
Contraindications: This procedure is not recommended for those with severe allergies to lidocaine, active skin infections, or certain autoimmune conditions. A thorough medical evaluation will determine your candidacy.

Recovery & Aftercare

First 24 Hours

  • Avoid touching or massaging treated areas
  • Apply ice packs to reduce swelling
  • Sleep elevated to minimize swelling
  • Avoid strenuous exercise and heat

First Week

  • Minor swelling and bruising may occur
  • Avoid intense workouts for 48 hours
  • Skip dental work for 2 weeks if lips treated
  • Use gentle skincare products

2 Weeks

  • Final results become apparent
  • Optional touch-up assessment
  • All activities can be resumed
  • Maintain results with good skincare

Long-Term Care

  • Schedule maintenance treatments every 12-18 months
  • Use daily SPF to protect results
  • Maintain healthy lifestyle for longevity
  • Consider complementary treatments

Aftercare Instructions

Ice: Apply cold compresses for 10 minutes hourly first day

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do Juvederm results last?

Results typically last 12-18 months depending on the product used and treatment area. Voluma for cheeks can last up to 2 years, while lip fillers may last 9-12 months.

Is Juvederm painful?

Most patients experience minimal discomfort. Juvederm contains built-in lidocaine for pain relief, and we apply topical numbing cream before treatment.

When will I see results?

Results are visible immediately after treatment. Final results become apparent after 2 weeks once any swelling subsides.

Can fillers look natural?

Absolutely! Our physicians use conservative techniques and advanced injection methods to create natural-looking enhancement that preserves your unique features.

What's the difference between Juvederm and Botox?

Botox relaxes muscles to smooth wrinkles, while Juvederm restores volume and fills static lines. They're often combined for complete rejuvenation.

Is Juvederm safe for dark skin?

Yes! Juvederm is safe and effective for all skin tones. Our physicians have extensive experience treating Caribbean patients with melanin-rich skin.

Can Juvederm be reversed?

Yes. Hyaluronic acid fillers like Juvederm can be dissolved with hyaluronidase if needed, though this is rarely necessary with skilled injectors.

How much does Juvederm cost in Jamaica?

Cost varies based on areas treated and amount of product needed. During consultation, we'll provide a detailed quote based on your goals.

Will my lips look overdone?

Our philosophy emphasizes natural enhancement. We use conservative techniques to create proportionate, beautiful results that suit your facial features.

When can I wear makeup after treatment?

You can apply makeup 24 hours after treatment once any redness subsides. Use gentle products and avoid rubbing treated areas.
