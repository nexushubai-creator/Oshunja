Overview

Botox Enhanced at OSHUNJA elevates the classic anti‑wrinkle treatment with precise facial mapping, micro‑dosing, and advanced placement techniques. The result is ultra‑natural softening of forehead lines, frown lines, and crow’s feet—without the “frozen” look.

Our Kingston aesthetic team tailors dosing to your muscle strength and expression patterns. Strategic placement maintains natural movement where you need it and relaxes overactive areas that crease the skin—so you look refreshed, not overdone.

This refined approach often extends result longevity and improves balance across the upper face. Many patients choose to pair Enhanced Botox with dermal fillers or skin treatments for comprehensive rejuvenation.

How It Works & What To Expect

1

Consultation & Facial Mapping

We review your goals and analyze muscle movement, symmetry, and line patterns. Photos may be taken to plan precise injection sites.

2

Preparation

Skin is cleansed; makeup removed. Optional topical numbing is available, though most patients find treatment very tolerable.

3

Micro‑Dosed Injections

Ultra‑fine needles deliver precisely measured doses to targeted muscles. Strategic placement softens lines while preserving natural expression.

4

Immediate Aftercare

Gentle pressure is applied. You’ll receive clear aftercare instructions and can resume normal activities immediately.

5

2‑Week Review & Optimization

We reassess at two weeks to fine‑tune symmetry and ensure balanced, natural results. Touch‑ups can be performed if needed.

Ideal Candidates & Considerations

Enhanced Botox May Be Right For You If:

  • You want smoother forehead, frown lines, or crow’s feet—without a “frozen” look
  • Your work or lifestyle benefits from subtle, natural‑looking results
  • You prefer a refined approach with precise mapping and micro‑dosing
  • You’re ready to maintain results every 3–4 months
  • You are in good general health and have realistic expectations

Important Safety Considerations:

  • Contraindications: Pregnancy/breastfeeding, neuromuscular disorders, allergy to botulinum toxin
  • Medications: Blood thinners may increase bruising—timing adjustments may help
  • Static vs Dynamic: Deep static lines may also need fillers for optimal smoothing
  • Expectations: Results are gradual over days, not instant
Note: We customize your plan based on anatomy and goals, and we’ll advise if complementary treatments can enhance your outcome.

Recovery & Aftercare

First 24 Hours

  • Avoid lying flat for 4 hours
  • No rubbing or massaging treated areas
  • Skip strenuous exercise and alcohol
  • Stay upright to prevent product migration

First Week

  • Initial results appear within 3–5 days
  • Mild redness or pinpoint marks may occur
  • Avoid facials or aggressive treatments for one week
  • Protect skin with daily SPF

2 Weeks

  • Full results visible
  • Attend review appointment for fine‑tuning if needed

Aftercare Instructions

Activity: Avoid strenuous workouts for 24 hours
Position: Remain upright for 4 hours post‑treatment
Skincare: No rubbing/massage over treated areas for 24 hours
Sun: Daily SPF 30+ in Jamaica’s climate
Follow‑up: 2‑week review to optimize results
When to Contact Us: Severe headache, vision changes, difficulty swallowing, breathing problems, or unusual muscle weakness require immediate attention.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Enhanced Botox different from regular Botox?

Enhanced Botox uses precise facial mapping and micro‑dosing to preserve natural expression while softening lines. It’s a more tailored approach than standard patterns.

When will I see results and how long do they last?

Expect visible changes in 3–5 days with full effect at two weeks. Results typically last 3–6 months depending on muscle strength and metabolism.

Will I look frozen?

No. Our mapping and micro‑dosing are designed to preserve natural movement while reducing unwanted creasing.

Is treatment painful?

Most patients report minimal discomfort—brief pinches. Ultra‑fine needles and optional numbing maximize comfort.

Can I combine this with fillers or PRP?

Absolutely. Fillers address static lines and volume; PRP and microneedling enhance skin quality. Together they provide comprehensive rejuvenation.

How do I reduce bruising risk?

Avoid alcohol and non‑prescribed blood thinners for 24 hours before and after if possible; follow our pre‑treatment guidance.

Is this safe for darker skin?

Yes. Botox is safe across all skin tones. Our physicians have extensive experience treating melanin‑rich Caribbean skin.

How much does Enhanced Botox cost in Jamaica?

Pricing depends on the areas treated and units used. We’ll provide a personalized quote during consultation and discuss package options.
