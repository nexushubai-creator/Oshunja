Overview

Botox Luxury at OSHUNJA refines the classic anti‑wrinkle treatment with extended consultation, precise facial mapping, and micro‑dosed placement for a soft, natural finish. Our concierge approach emphasizes comfort, privacy, and subtle enhancement—never overdone.

Treat common areas like the forehead, frown lines (glabella), and crow’s feet while maintaining authentic expression. Your plan is tailored to your muscle strength and movement for balanced results that complement your features.

Many patients combine Luxury Botox with fillers or skin therapies for comprehensive rejuvenation and a polished, refreshed look.

How It Works & What To Expect

1

Consultation & Goals

Discuss your priorities and evaluate expression patterns, symmetry, and line depth. Photos may be taken to plan precise injection sites.

2

Facial Mapping & Plan

We map overactive muscles and design a bespoke dosing strategy to soften lines while preserving natural movement.

3

Preparation

Skin is cleansed and makeup removed. Optional topical numbing is available though most patients find treatment very tolerable.

4

Precision Micro‑Dosed Injections

Ultra‑fine needles deliver measured doses at strategic points to relax targeted muscles and smooth creases naturally.

5

2‑Week Review & Optimization

We reassess at two weeks to fine‑tune symmetry and ensure balanced, refined results. Touch‑ups can be performed if needed.

Ideal Candidates & Considerations

Luxury Botox May Be Right For You If:

  • You want smoother forehead, frown lines, or crow’s feet—without a “frozen” look
  • You value a premium experience with privacy, comfort, and priority follow‑up
  • You prefer bespoke mapping and micro‑dosing for refined, natural results
  • You’re ready to maintain results every 3–4 months
  • You are in good general health and have realistic expectations

Important Safety Considerations:

  • Contraindications: Pregnancy/breastfeeding, neuromuscular disorders, allergy to botulinum toxin
  • Medications: Blood thinners may increase bruising—timing adjustments may help
  • Static vs Dynamic: Deep static lines may also need fillers for optimal smoothing
  • Expectations: Results are gradual over days, not instant
Note: Your plan is customized to anatomy and goals. We’ll advise if complementary treatments (fillers, PRP, or skin therapies) can enhance your outcome.

Recovery & Aftercare

First 24 Hours

  • Avoid lying flat for 4 hours
  • No rubbing or massaging treated areas
  • Skip strenuous exercise and alcohol
  • Stay upright to prevent product migration

First Week

  • Initial results appear within 3–5 days
  • Mild redness or pinpoint marks may occur
  • Avoid facials or aggressive treatments for one week
  • Protect skin with daily SPF

2 Weeks

  • Full results visible
  • Attend review appointment for fine‑tuning if needed

Aftercare Instructions

Activity: Avoid strenuous workouts for 24 hours
Position: Remain upright for 4 hours post‑treatment
Skincare: No rubbing/massage over treated areas for 24 hours
Sun: Daily SPF 30+ in Jamaica’s climate
Follow‑up: 2‑week review to optimize results
When to Contact Us: Severe headache, vision changes, difficulty swallowing, breathing problems, or unusual muscle weakness require immediate attention.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Luxury Botox different from regular Botox?

Luxury Botox features extended consultation, precise facial mapping, and micro‑dosing for refined, natural results—plus priority aftercare and a 2‑week optimization visit.

When will I see results and how long do they last?

Expect visible changes in 3–5 days with full effect at two weeks. Results typically last 3–6 months depending on muscle strength and metabolism.

Will I look frozen?

No. Our mapping and micro‑dosing preserve your natural expression while softening unwanted creasing.

Is treatment painful?

Most patients report minimal discomfort—brief pinches. Ultra‑fine needles and optional numbing maximize comfort.

Can I combine this with fillers or PRP?

Yes. Fillers address static lines and volume; PRP and microneedling enhance skin quality. Together they provide comprehensive rejuvenation.

How do I reduce bruising risk?

Avoid alcohol and non‑prescribed blood thinners for 24 hours before and after if possible; follow our pre‑treatment guidance.

Is this safe for darker skin?

Yes. Botox is safe across all skin tones. Our physicians have extensive experience treating melanin‑rich Caribbean skin.

How many units will I need?

Units vary by area, muscle strength, and goals. We’ll recommend a personalized plan after assessing your facial movement.

Can I work out or travel after treatment?

Resume light activities immediately. Avoid strenuous exercise and lying flat for 4 hours; flying later the same day is typically fine.
