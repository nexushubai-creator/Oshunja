FAQ

Common Questions

How long does Botox last?

Botox typically lasts 3-4 months. Results vary by individual based on metabolism, muscle strength, and lifestyle factors. With regular treatments, many patients find results last longer as muscles become trained to relax.

Does Botox hurt?

Most patients describe the sensation as a quick pinch. We use ultra-fine needles to minimize discomfort. The entire procedure takes just 15-30 minutes, and numbing cream is available if desired, though most patients don't require it.

Will I look frozen or unnatural?

Not when performed by our experienced physicians. We use precise dosing and strategic placement to smooth wrinkles while maintaining natural facial expressions. You'll look refreshed and rejuvenated, not frozen.

When will I see results?

You'll begin to notice results in 3-5 days as the Botox takes effect. Full results are visible at 2 weeks. We offer a complimentary follow-up appointment to ensure you're completely satisfied.

Is Botox safe for all skin tones?

Yes. Botox works on muscles, not skin pigmentation, making it safe and effective for all skin tones including melanin-rich Caribbean skin. Our physicians have extensive experience treating diverse patients in Kingston.

How much does Botox cost in Jamaica?

Pricing varies based on the number of treatment areas and units required. During your complimentary consultation, we'll provide a detailed quote tailored to your needs. We offer flexible payment plans to make treatment accessible.
