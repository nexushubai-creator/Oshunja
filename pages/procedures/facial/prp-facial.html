Overview

The PRP Facial at OSHUNJA combines microneedling with platelet-rich plasma (PRP) extracted from your own blood. This powerful treatment stimulates collagen production, improves skin texture, reduces fine lines, and creates a natural glow‚Äîusing your body's own healing growth factors.

In Kingston's sun-rich environment, skin can show premature aging from UV exposure. Our PRP facials are particularly effective for Caribbean skin, addressing concerns like hyperpigmentation, sun damage, and uneven tone while promoting cellular regeneration and healing.

The treatment involves drawing a small amount of blood, isolating the concentrated platelets, then applying this serum to your skin during microneedling. The result is accelerated healing, enhanced collagen synthesis, and remarkable skin transformation over the following months.

How It Works & What To Expect

1

Blood Draw & PRP Preparation

A small amount of blood (similar to routine lab work) is drawn from your arm. The sample is placed in a centrifuge to separate and concentrate the platelet-rich plasma containing powerful growth factors.

2

Skin Preparation & Numbing

Your face is thoroughly cleansed and a topical numbing cream is applied for 20-30 minutes. We want you comfortable throughout the microneedling portion of the treatment.

3

Microneedling with PRP Application

Using a sterile microneedling device, we create thousands of tiny micro-channels in the skin. Your concentrated PRP is simultaneously applied and pressed deep into the dermis, where growth factors stimulate collagen and healing.

4

Post-Treatment Care & Recovery

Any remaining PRP is applied as a serum to the skin surface. We provide detailed aftercare instructions, and you'll leave with some redness‚Äîlike a mild sunburn‚Äîthat fades within 2-3 days.

5

Series & Follow‚ÄëUp

Most patients complete 3‚Äì4 sessions spaced 4‚Äì6 weeks apart. We reassess progress and plan maintenance for lasting glow and collagen support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PRP facial painful?

Most patients describe mild to moderate discomfort during microneedling. We apply topical numbing cream 30-45 minutes before treatment to minimize discomfort. You may feel a slight scratching sensation and pressure, but it's generally well-tolerated. After treatment, your skin may feel sunburned and tender for 24-48 hours.

How long do PRP facial results last?

Initial results appear within 2-4 weeks and continue improving for 2-3 months as collagen remodeling occurs. With proper skincare and sun protection, results typically last 6-12 months. Most patients schedule a series of 3-4 treatments spaced 4-6 weeks apart for optimal results, followed by maintenance treatments every 6-12 months.

When can I wear makeup after PRP facial?

Wait at least 24-48 hours before applying makeup to allow microchannels to close properly and reduce infection risk. When you resume makeup, use clean, mineral-based products. Avoid heavy foundation or products with harsh chemicals for the first week. Most patients feel comfortable returning to makeup on day 3 when redness has significantly subsided.

Can I do PRP facial if I have active acne?

Not during active breakouts. If you have active acne, inflamed pustules, or open lesions at treatment sites, we'll need to postpone until skin is clear to prevent spreading bacteria and worsening inflammation. However, PRP facial can actually help improve acne scars, hyperpigmentation from past acne, and overall skin texture once active acne is controlled.

How many treatments do I need?

Most patients achieve optimal results with a series of 3-4 treatments spaced 4-6 weeks apart. This allows time for collagen remodeling between sessions. Some see noticeable improvement after just one treatment, but a series produces more dramatic, lasting results. After your initial series, maintenance treatments every 6-12 months help sustain results.

Is PRP facial safe for dark skin and melanin-rich skin?

Absolutely! PRP facial is actually an excellent option for darker skin tones and Caribbean skin because it works with your body's natural healing response rather than using harsh chemicals or heat that can cause hyperpigmentation. Our Kingston team has extensive experience treating melanin-rich skin and uses techniques specifically tailored to minimize any risk of post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation.

What's the difference between PRP facial and regular microneedling?

Regular microneedling creates microchannels to stimulate collagen but doesn't add anything to the skin. PRP facial combines microneedling with platelet-rich plasma‚Äîyour own concentrated growth factors‚Äîwhich supercharges the healing response. The PRP infuses into microchannels, delivering powerful growth factors directly to deeper skin layers. This combination produces more dramatic results than microneedling alone, especially for texture, tone, and anti-aging benefits.

Can I get PRP facial before a special event?

Plan for at least 4-6 weeks before important events. You'll need 3-5 days for visible redness to resolve, and optimal results don't appear until 4-6 weeks post-treatment as collagen remodels. If you have an event coming up, schedule your final treatment in a series 6-8 weeks before so you look your absolute best. Never schedule your first PRP treatment right before a wedding or major event.

Can I combine PRP facial with Botox or fillers?

Yes, but timing matters! Most providers recommend waiting 2 weeks after Botox or fillers before doing PRP facial to avoid disrupting product placement. Alternatively, some practitioners perform Botox/fillers 2 weeks after PRP once skin has healed. During your consultation, we'll create a strategic treatment plan if you're interested in combining procedures for comprehensive facial rejuvenation.

What should I avoid after PRP facial treatment?

For the first 24-48 hours: no makeup, intense exercise, swimming, saunas, hot tubs, or direct sun exposure. For one week: avoid harsh skincare (retinols, acids, exfoliants), excessive heat, and unprotected sun exposure. For two weeks: wear SPF 50+ religiously and avoid tanning. Also avoid blood-thinning medications or supplements (aspirin, ibuprofen, fish oil) for 3 days before and after treatment to minimize bruising, unless medically necessary.

How much does PRP facial cost in Jamaica?

Cost varies based on treatment area and whether it's part of a series. During your consultation at OSHUNJA in Kingston, we'll provide detailed pricing for single treatments and package rates for series of 3-4 sessions (which offer better value). We also offer flexible payment plans to make treatment more accessible.
