Overview

Glow with PRP Facial Rejuvenation

PRP facial rejuvenation layers platelet-rich plasma over freshly microneedled skin to deliver growth factors deep into the dermis.

The treatment enhances collagen, elasticity, and circulation for a lifted, radiant complexion. We tailor depth and serum mixes to address scars, enlarged pores, or dullness while preserving the integrity of melanated skin.

Post-care includes cooling therapy and antioxidant shields suited for the Caribbean climate.

FAQ

Common Questions

How many PRP facials do I need?

A series of three treatments spaced four weeks apart is typical, followed by maintenance every six months for optimal long-term results.

Is there downtime?

Expect two days of redness. Most clients resume social plans within 48 hours. Makeup can be applied after 24 hours.

Can PRP be combined with Botox or fillers?

Yes. We often layer neuromodulators or fillers two weeks before or after PRP for comprehensive rejuvenation without compromising results.
