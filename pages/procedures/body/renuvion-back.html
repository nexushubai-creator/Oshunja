Overview

Renuvion Back at OSHUNJA provides minimally invasive tightening care for your back with concierge preparation, treatment, and follow-up.

Each visit includes personalised education, imaging or laboratory review where appropriate, and coordination with our wellness team so results integrate seamlessly into Kingston life.

Procedure Details

  • Private consultation reviews goals, medical history, and lifestyle considerations.
  • Our clinicians deliver minimally invasive tightening care with comfort measures tailored to your back.
  • Post-treatment soothing or stabilisation is provided before you transition to recovery.
  • We lock in follow-up milestones, digital touchpoints, and supportive resources before you depart.

Anesthesia: Local anaesthesia with sedation

Duration: Around 90 minutes

Immediate Aftercare

  • Follow the personalised home-care plan and check-in schedule provided by your clinician.
  • Stay hydrated and nourish the body with balanced meals to support healing.
  • Use prescribed topicals, garments, or medications exactly as directed to protect results.

Possible Side Effects & Precautions

  • Temporary tenderness or swelling in the treated area is normal and typically resolves within a few days.
  • Minor bruising or redness can appear depending on your skin type and the intensity of treatment.
  • Contact our team immediately if you notice unexpected discomfort, fever, or changes in sensation.

Benefits & Results

  • Delivers focused minimally invasive tightening care for the back with physician oversight.
  • Integrates concierge follow-up so improvements are maintained long term.
  • Pairs easily with complementary OSHUNJA services for whole-body harmony.

Timeline: 3 to 12 months

Candidates & Considerations

Ideal Candidates

  • Clients seeking expert, evidence-based care with personalised attention.
  • Individuals who can commit to recommended follow-up and lifestyle guidance.
  • Those wanting to address concerns proactively rather than waiting for advanced symptoms.

Important Considerations

  • Share full medical history, medications, and supplements so protocols can be tailored safely.
  • Some systemic conditions may require coordination with your primary physician before treatment.
  • Pregnancy and breastfeeding may necessitate postponement depending on the procedure.

Frequently Asked Questions

How soon will I notice changes?

Most guests notice initial improvements within the first few weeks, with optimal outcomes aligning with the 3 to 12 months timeline.

What should I do to prepare?

Our team provides a preparation checklist covering hydration, medications, and lifestyle adjustments one to two weeks prior.

Will I need multiple sessions?

We personalise treatment frequency; your provider will outline whether a series, annual visits, or maintenance is recommended.
