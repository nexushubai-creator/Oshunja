Silkier Arms With Renuvion

Tighten post-weight-loss or post-pregnancy laxity along the arms using helium plasma skin contraction.

Overview

Renuvion arms contracts collagen beneath the skin, reducing wobble and crepe texture along the upper arms. It is ideal for clients who notice laxity despite gym routines, especially after weight changes or pregnancy.

Our surgeons often pair Renuvion with micro-liposuction to remove residual fat before tightening the skin envelope. The procedure respects Caribbean lifestyles, offering a swift recovery so you can return to sleeveless wardrobes and wellness routines quickly.

Meticulous temperature monitoring and surgeon expertise ensure safety while delivering long-lasting arm definition without the scars of an arm lift.

Procedure Details

  • Local anaesthetic is applied and tiny entry points are created near the elbow and underarm.
  • Micro-cannulas remove stubborn fat if present, sculpting the arm contour.
  • The Renuvion wand glides beneath the skin, delivering plasma energy that contracts tissue instantly.
  • Skin is cooled and dressed, then a compression sleeve is fitted before departure.
  • Follow-up visits document progress and adjust garment wear for optimal contraction.

Anesthesia: Tumescent local anaesthetic with optional oral sedation keeps you relaxed.

Duration: Around 90 minutes for both arms; add time if liposuction is performed.

Immediate Aftercare

  • Wear compression sleeves for four weeks, removing only for bathing and lymphatic massage.
  • Limit heavy lifting and upper-body workouts for three weeks to protect healing tissue.
  • Drink plenty of water and reduce salt to control swelling in Jamaica's warm climate.
  • Protect entry points from sun exposure using breathable clothing or SPF once healed.

Possible Side Effects & Precautions

  • Swelling and tingling may persist for several weeks as nerves recover.
  • Bruising and firmness along the tract are common and soften with massage.
  • Temporary numb patches typically resolve over a few months.
  • Thermal injury risk is very low with experienced surgeons and real-time temperature monitoring (Aesthetic Surgery Journal).

Benefits & Results

  • Smooths and tightens upper arm skin without long incisions.
  • Contours stubborn fat pockets when combined with micro-liposuction.
  • Supports natural collagen rebuilding for lasting firmness.
  • Minimal downtime compared with brachioplasty surgery.

Timeline: Early tightening is visible within four weeks; final collagen contraction completes between three and twelve months.

Candidates & Considerations

Ideal Candidates

  • Individuals with mild to moderate loose skin who prefer non-surgical options.
  • Post-weight-loss clients looking to refine tone without long scars.
  • Those committed to wearing compression and maintaining stable weight.

Important Considerations

  • Severe laxity with significant skin excess may require surgical arm lift; we will advise during consultation.
  • Smoking compromises healing; abstain for six weeks before and after surgery.
  • Discuss any history of lymphedema or clotting disorders to ensure suitability.

Complementary Treatments

  • Liposuction 360 / 180Combine with torso sculpting for cohesive silhouette refinement.
  • Cold PlungeEnhance lymphatic flow during recovery with guided cold immersion.
  • Wellness CoachingStay accountable with nutrition and movement plans that protect your results.

Caribbean Care Notes

  • Choose breathable compression garments suited to Jamaica's humidity to prevent overheating.
  • Schedule early morning walks to keep circulation moving without exposing healing skin to direct midday sun.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will I see final Renuvion arm results?

Expect gradual improvements over three to six months as collagen tightens.

Will there be scars?

Only rice-sized entry points that fade to near-invisible with proper care.

Can I shower after surgery?

You may shower after 48 hours once incisions are sealed; pat dry and reapply compression sleeves.
