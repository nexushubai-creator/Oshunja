Overview

Renuvion is a minimally invasive skin tightening procedure that combines radiofrequency energy with helium plasma to contract collagen instantly while stimulating long-term remodeling. OSHUNJA deploys Renuvion along the neck and jawline to address laxity, tech neck bands, and early jowling without extended downtime.

Performed by our board-certified surgeons, the procedure pairs well with liposuction or can be a stand-alone rejuvenation for clients who notice neck skin softening due to age, weight changes, or Caribbean sun exposure.

Every treatment plan includes lymphatic support, scar management, and follow-up imaging so you can visualise progress over the 12-month collagen maturation period.

Procedure Details

  • Local anaesthetic and light sedation keep you comfortable while micro entry points are created under the chin and behind the ears.
  • A slim Renuvion wand delivers controlled plasma energy beneath the skin, contracting tissue and stimulating collagen.
  • We sculpt along the platysma bands and jawline for even tightening while protecting surrounding structures.
  • A supportive compression garment is fitted before you leave to encourage a crisp contour.
  • Post-operative check-ins occur at 48 hours, two weeks, and three months to monitor healing and results.

Anesthesia: Local anaesthesia with oral or twilight sedation; general anaesthesia is rarely required.

Duration: 60 to 90 minutes depending on combination procedures.

Immediate Aftercare

  • Wear the provided chin sling day and night for the first 72 hours, then at night for two weeks.
  • Sleep elevated on two pillows to minimise swelling and protect the treated area.
  • Avoid beach outings, saunas, and midday heat during the first week.
  • Begin gentle lymphatic massage after seven days if cleared by your surgeon.

Possible Side Effects & Precautions

  • Swelling, firmness, and mild numbness can last several weeks as tissues remodel.
  • Bruising is common and resolves within 10 to 14 days; arnica and bromelain can help.
  • Temporary contour irregularities improve as swelling dissipates.
  • Rare risks include burns or prolonged nerve irritation; strict temperature monitoring keeps rates extremely low (Journal of Cosmetic Dermatology).

Benefits & Results

  • Contours the jawline and tightens lax neck skin without a full surgical lift.
  • Stimulates collagen for progressive improvement up to 12 months.
  • Pairs seamlessly with liposuction for sculpted 360° definition.
  • Small entry points result in minimal scarring and downtime.

Timeline: Expect visible tightening by six weeks, continued refinement through month six, and collagen maturation up to one year.

Candidates & Considerations

Ideal Candidates

  • Clients noticing crepey neck skin, early jowls, or tech neck lines.
  • Those seeking lift without a traditional neck lift or extended recovery.
  • Individuals who maintain stable weight and healthy lifestyle habits.

Important Considerations

  • Not suitable during pregnancy, breastfeeding, or for those with uncontrolled thyroid or bleeding disorders.
  • Smoking can impair healing; abstain for four weeks before and after the procedure.
  • Discuss prior surgery or keloid history so we can tailor incision care.

Complementary Treatments

  • Liposuction 360 / 180Remove submental fat while Renuvion tightens the skin envelope.
  • Renuvion AbdomenExtend collagen tightening through the torso for cohesive results.
  • IV TherapySupport recovery with hydration and collagen-building nutrients.

Caribbean Care Notes

  • Wear breathable scarves or SPF-protective clothing outdoors during the first month to shield healing skin from UV exposure.
  • Stay hydrated with coconut water and limit sodium-rich festive meals to reduce swelling.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do Renuvion neck results last?

Results can last several years when paired with healthy habits and sun protection; collagen continues to remodel for 12 months.

Will I have scars?

Entry points are about 3 mm and hidden under the chin or behind the ear; they fade to near-invisible with proper care.

Can Renuvion be done with liposuction?

Yes, combining submental liposuction with Renuvion refines fat and tightens skin in one appointment.

When can I work out again?

Gentle walks are encouraged immediately; resume gym workouts at three weeks unless otherwise directed.
