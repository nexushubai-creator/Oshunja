How It Works & What To Expect

1

Comprehensive Consultation

Meet with our Kingston surgeon to evaluate your skin laxity, discuss treatment areas, and set realistic expectations. We'll assess skin quality, determine if you're a good candidate, and create your personalized Renuvion treatment plan.

2

Pre-Procedure Preparation

Arrive at our Kingston facility on procedure day. Anesthesia options (local with sedation or general) are reviewed. Treatment areas are marked and photographed for before/after comparison.

3

Renuvion Treatment

Tiny incisions (3-5mm) are made in discreet locations. The Renuvion handpiece is inserted beneath the skin, delivering controlled helium plasma and RF energy. This heats subdermal tissue to precise temperatures, causing immediate collagen contraction. Multiple passes ensure uniform tightening across treatment zones.

4

Immediate Recovery

Incisions are closed with tiny sutures or skin glue. Compression garments are applied to treated areas. You rest in recovery for 1-2 hours before being discharged home the same day with detailed aftercare instructions.

Ideal Candidates & Considerations

You May Be an Ideal Candidate If:

  • You're within 20 to 30 pounds of your goal weight seeking targeted contouring
  • You're postpartum or post-weight-loss with good skin elasticity
  • You have stubborn fat deposits that resist diet and exercise
  • You're committed to healthy nutrition and movement post-procedure
  • You're in good overall health with no major medical conditions
  • You have realistic expectations about the procedure and results
  • You're a non-smoker or willing to quit before and after surgery

Important Considerations:

  • Weight: Not a weight-loss procedure; we will recommend metabolic support if BMI is above 32
  • Smoking: Must cease six weeks before and after to reduce healing complications
  • Medical Conditions: Uncontrolled diabetes requires optimisation with our wellness team first
  • Skin Elasticity: Adequate skin elasticity needed for optimal contouring results
  • Recovery Time: Plan to remain in Kingston for at least seven days for follow-ups and lymphatic care
  • Compression Garments: Must commit to wearing compression 24/7 for six weeks
Contraindications: This procedure is not recommended for those with uncontrolled medical conditions, active infections, or certain blood clotting disorders. A thorough medical evaluation will determine your candidacy.

Recovery & Aftercare

First 24-48 Hours

  • Rest in climate-controlled spaces; humidity can intensify swelling
  • Wear compression garments 24/7 as directed
  • Begin gentle lymphatic drainage within 72 hours
  • Walk every few hours while awake to support circulation
  • Take prescribed pain medication as needed

First Week

  • Continue wearing compression garments constantly
  • Attend follow-up appointments as scheduled
  • Desk work usually resumes in 7 to 10 days
  • Avoid beaches, pools, and hot tubs until cleared
  • Light walking is encouraged for circulation

2-6 Weeks

  • Swelling continues to decrease gradually
  • More active roles may resume after three weeks
  • Maintain compression garments for full six weeks
  • Continue lymphatic massage sessions as recommended

6 Weeks to 6 Months

  • Switch to lighter support garments at week seven
  • Gradually resume all normal activities and exercise
  • Final contour reveals around month three
  • Tissues continue settling through month six

Aftercare Instructions

Compression: Wear garments 24/7 for six weeks; vital for shaping and controlling swelling
Activity: Walk regularly but avoid strenuous exercise until cleared by your surgeon
Hydration: Drink plenty of water and reduce salt to control swelling in warm climate
Lymphatic Care: Begin gentle drainage within 72 hours to reduce swelling and fibrosis
Climate: Plan recovery in climate-controlled spaces; schedule garment fittings during cooler morning hours
Follow-up: Attend all scheduled check-ups to monitor healing and progress
When to Contact Us: Call immediately if you experience severe pain, excessive swelling, fever, signs of infection, or fluid collections (seromas).

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Renuvion differ from traditional surgical skin tightening?

Renuvion is minimally invasive with small incisions, less downtime, and faster recovery compared to traditional excisional surgery. It uses helium plasma and radiofrequency to tighten skin from beneath without large scars or extensive tissue removal.

Is Renuvion painful?

Most patients report mild to moderate soreness rather than severe pain. Local or general anesthesia is used during the procedure. Post-treatment discomfort is manageable with prescribed pain medication and typically resolves within 3-5 days.

When will I see results?

Initial tightening is visible immediately, but full results develop over 3-6 months as collagen remodels and contracts. Most dramatic improvement appears around month 3, with continued refinement through month 6.

How long do Renuvion results last?

Results can last 5-10 years with stable weight and healthy lifestyle. Natural aging continues, but treated skin remains tighter than it would without intervention. Many patients maintain results long-term with good skin care.

Do I need compression garments?

Yes, compression is essential for 4-6 weeks to support healing, reduce swelling, and optimize skin contraction. Garments should be worn 23 hours daily initially, gradually decreasing as directed by your surgeon.

Can Renuvion be combined with liposuction?

Absolutely! Renuvion is often performed with liposuction for optimal body contouring. Liposuction removes fat while Renuvion tightens remaining skin‚Äîthe combination prevents loose skin after fat removal and delivers superior sculpting results.

What's the recovery time?

Most patients return to desk work in 5-7 days. Swelling and bruising peak at day 3-4 and resolve over 2-3 weeks. Avoid strenuous exercise for 4-6 weeks. Full recovery and final results take 3-6 months.

Are there visible scars?

Incisions are tiny (5-8mm) and strategically placed in natural skin creases or hidden areas. With proper care, scars fade to barely noticeable marks within 6-12 months. Significantly less scarring than traditional surgical lifts.

How much does Renuvion cost in Jamaica?

Cost depends on treatment areas and whether combined with liposuction or other procedures. During your Kingston consultation, we'll provide detailed pricing based on your specific needs. Payment plans are available.

Is Renuvion safe for dark skin?

Yes! Renuvion is safe for all skin tones, including melanin-rich Caribbean skin. The technology works beneath the skin surface, avoiding hyperpigmentation risks associated with some laser treatments. Our surgeons specialize in treating darker skin types.
