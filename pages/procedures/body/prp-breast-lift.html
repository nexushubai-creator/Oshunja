Overview

The PRP breast lift is a regenerative procedure that enhances cleavage, improves skin tone, and heightens sensitivity using your own platelet-rich plasma. It is ideal for clients noticing volume loss after breastfeeding, weight changes, or ageing who prefer a non-surgical approach.

During the appointment we combine PRP injections with collagen-building dermal threads or microneedling across stretch marks when appropriate. The result is a subtle lift, improved projection, and smoother décolletage while preserving a natural look for Caribbean wardrobes.

Our Kingston clinicians emphasise safety, privacy, and customised plans that may include hormone optimisation or supportive garments for long-lasting outcomes.

Procedure Details

  • A small blood sample is processed to concentrate growth factors in platelet-rich plasma.
  • Topical and local anaesthetic keep the chest comfortable during treatment.
  • PRP is injected strategically along the upper breast, cleavage line, and stretch marks to stimulate collagen.
  • Optional microneedling or PDO threads enhance lift and texture if indicated.
  • Post-procedure LED therapy calms the skin before you are fitted with a supportive bralette.

Anesthesia: Topical numbing and local anaesthetic injections ensure the session remains comfortable.

Duration: 60 to 75 minutes including blood processing and aftercare.

Immediate Aftercare

  • Wear the recommended soft support bra day and night for three days.
  • Avoid high-impact workouts, ocean swims, and saunas for five days.
  • Sleep on your back for the first three nights to protect injection sites.
  • Moisturise the décolletage with provided serums and apply SPF daily to protect results.

Possible Side Effects & Precautions

  • Bruising and soreness can last up to one week, similar to an intense workout.
  • Swelling may create temporary fullness before settling into final shape.
  • Because PRP is autologous, allergic reactions are extremely rare (Cleveland Clinic).
  • Small nodules may form if aftercare instructions are not followed; gentle massage typically resolves them.

Benefits & Results

  • Enhances cleavage and upper pole fullness without implants.
  • Improves nipple sensitivity and skin texture.
  • Reduces the appearance of stretch marks and décolletage creasing.
  • No general anaesthesia or surgical scars.

Timeline: Swelling settles within one week, and collagen remodeling delivers noticeable lift over 6 to 12 weeks; annual touch-ups maintain results.

Candidates & Considerations

Ideal Candidates

  • Clients with mild deflation or asymmetry after breastfeeding or weight change.
  • Individuals seeking natural improvement without implants or surgical downtime.
  • Those with good skin quality willing to commit to supportive bras and follow-up sessions.

Important Considerations

  • Not recommended during pregnancy, breastfeeding, or for individuals with clotting disorders.
  • Results are subtle compared to surgical lifts; expectation setting is part of every consultation.
  • Hormonal imbalances may influence outcome; coordinate with our wellness team if needed.

  • Apply mineral sunscreen across the chest daily to preserve pigment evenness in tropical sun.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the PRP breast lift last?

Most guests schedule maintenance once a year; collagen gains can last longer with healthy habits.

Can PRP increase breast size?

Expect subtle fullness and lift rather than a full cup-size change; it is ideal for contour and skin quality.

Is there downtime?

You can resume desk work immediately but avoid heavy exercise and swimming for five days.

Will it affect breastfeeding later?

There is no evidence PRP injections harm future breastfeeding, but we wait until you are finished nursing to treat.
