Overview

The PRP Breast Lift at OSHUNJA offers non-surgical breast enhancement by injecting platelet-rich plasma to improve skin texture, increase subtle volume, and enhance cleavage appearance. This natural approach uses your body's growth factors to rejuvenate breast tissue without implants or surgery.

For Kingston women seeking subtle enhancement without downtime, the PRP breast lift provides a natural alternative. The treatment improves skin quality, adds mild volume, and creates a more youthful appearance‚Äîperfect for addressing minor sagging, enhancing cleavage, or improving skin tone.

We extract and concentrate platelets from your blood, then strategically inject them into breast tissue. The growth factors stimulate collagen production and tissue regeneration for gradual, natural-looking improvements over several months.

How It Works & What To Expect

1

Consultation & Blood Draw

We assess your breast concerns, discuss realistic expectations for non-surgical enhancement, and determine if PRP breast lift is appropriate for you. A small blood sample is drawn and processed in a centrifuge to isolate platelet-rich plasma.

2

Preparation & Numbing

Treatment areas are cleansed and marked. Topical numbing cream is applied for comfort during injections. Your concentrated PRP is prepared with growth factors ready for injection.

3

Strategic PRP Injection

Using fine needles, PRP is injected strategically into breast tissue at multiple points. We focus on the upper pole for subtle lift, d√©colletage for skin improvement, and areas needing volume. Optional hyaluronic acid filler may be combined for enhanced immediate results.

4

Post-Treatment Care

You'll rest briefly then receive detailed aftercare instructions. Mild swelling and tenderness are normal for 2-3 days. Results develop gradually over 2-3 months as collagen regeneration occurs.

5

Follow-Up & Optimization

We reassess at 6-8 weeks and may recommend a second treatment session for optimal results. Most patients complete 2-3 treatments spaced 2-3 months apart for best outcomes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much larger will my breasts get with PRP?

PRP breast lift provides subtle enhancement‚ÄîNOT significant size increase. Most patients see improved upper pole fullness and better cleavage, but volume gain is modest (typically less than half a cup size). This is not a replacement for breast implants if you want dramatic size increase.

How long do PRP breast lift results last?

Results typically last 12-18 months. As your own platelets stimulate natural collagen production, effects gradually fade over time. Most patients schedule touch-up treatments every 12-18 months to maintain their enhanced appearance.

Is PRP breast lift painful?

Most patients report mild discomfort during injections despite topical numbing. Tenderness and sensitivity may persist for 2-3 days post-treatment but is manageable with over-the-counter pain relievers. The procedure is far less painful than surgical breast augmentation or lift.

Can PRP lift sagging breasts?

PRP provides very modest lifting effect‚Äîideal for mild sagging or loss of upper pole fullness. Moderate to severe sagging requires surgical mastopexy (breast lift). During consultation, we'll honestly assess whether PRP can achieve your goals or if surgery is more appropriate.

How many treatments will I need?

Most patients achieve best results with 2-3 treatments spaced 2-3 months apart. This allows time for collagen regeneration between sessions. Some patients maintain results with annual touch-ups; others prefer more frequent treatments.

Can I combine PRP with breast implants or surgery?

Yes. Some patients use PRP breast lift to enhance skin quality before or after breast augmentation or lift surgery. PRP can improve healing, skin texture, and overall aesthetic results when combined with surgical procedures. Timing depends on your specific treatment plan.

Will PRP interfere with breastfeeding in the future?

PRP injections don't damage milk ducts or glandular tissue, so future breastfeeding ability shouldn't be affected. However, we don't treat patients who are currently pregnant or nursing. Wait until breastfeeding is complete before pursuing PRP breast treatments.

Does PRP breast lift show up on mammograms?

No. PRP doesn't create calcifications or abnormalities visible on mammograms. However, always inform your radiologist about any breast treatments you've received. PRP doesn't interfere with breast cancer screening.

What's the difference between PRP and filler for breasts?

PRP stimulates your body's own collagen production using growth factors from your blood‚Äîresults are gradual and natural. Hyaluronic acid fillers provide immediate volume but are absorbed over 6-12 months. Some providers combine both for immediate and long-term benefits.

Can PRP improve breast skin texture and stretch marks?

Yes! PRP is excellent for improving breast skin quality‚Äîreducing crepiness, fine lines, and the appearance of stretch marks. Collagen stimulation creates firmer, smoother, more youthful-looking breast skin even without significant volume changes.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How soon can I travel after liposuction 360?

    Plan to remain in Kingston for at least seven days for follow-ups and lymphatic care. This ensures optimal healing and allows our team to monitor your progress during the critical early recovery period.

    When can I return to work?

    Desk work usually resumes in 7 to 10 days; more active roles may need three weeks. We provide work notes as needed and will guide you based on your specific job requirements and healing progress.

    Do I have to wear compression?

    Yes. Continuous compression is vital for shaping and controlling swelling for six weeks. The compression garment supports your new contours and significantly improves your final results.

    Will fat come back?

    Removed fat cells do not return, but remaining cells can grow if weight increases, so maintenance is key. With a stable weight and healthy lifestyle, your results will be long-lasting.

    How much fat can be removed safely?

    Safety guidelines typically limit removal to 5 liters or about 5% of body weight. Our surgeons prioritize your safety and will determine the appropriate amount based on your anatomy and health status.

    Is liposuction 360 painful?

    Most patients describe moderate discomfort rather than pain, easily managed with prescribed medication. The tumescent technique used helps minimize post-operative discomfort.

    Can this replace diet and exercise?

    No, this is not a weight-loss procedure. It's designed for body contouring and removing stubborn fat pockets that resist diet and exercise. Best results come when you're near your goal weight.

    What about scarring?

    Incisions are tiny (3-5mm) and strategically placed to be inconspicuous. With proper care, they fade to barely visible marks within 6-12 months.

    How much does liposuction 360 cost in Jamaica?

    The cost varies depending on the extent of treatment and whether it's combined with other procedures. During your consultation, we'll provide a detailed quote. We offer flexible payment plans to make treatment accessible.

    Is the procedure safe for dark skin?

    Absolutely. Our surgeons have extensive experience treating patients of all skin tones, including melanin-rich Caribbean skin. We use techniques specifically tailored to optimize healing for darker skin types.
