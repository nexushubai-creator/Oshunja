Overview

Liposuction 360 treats the abdomen, waist, flanks, and back in one session to create a smooth, snatched silhouette. OSHUNJA utilises power-assisted cannulas and vibration technology to minimise trauma while maximising precision.

Our surgeons map each torso individually, considering posture, pelvic tilt, and Caribbean lifestyle habits to deliver results that look balanced in athleisure, beachwear, and tailored fashion.

Recovery is supported by onsite lymphatic specialists, IV therapy, and compression fittings, ensuring you feel cared for from consultation through final reveal.

Procedure Details

  • Marked zones are numbed with tumescent anaesthetic that also constricts blood vessels.
  • Micro-cannulas remove fat circumferentially around the torso to sculpt an hourglass or V-taper.
  • Fat is feathered into adjacent zones for smooth transitions; transferred fat can enhance hips if desired.
  • Incisions are closed with absorbable sutures or left to drain depending on treatment plan.
  • You are placed in a custom compression garment and escorted to a private recovery lounge.

Anesthesia: Twilight sedation or general anaesthesia depending on the extent of contouring.

Duration: 2.5 to 4 hours based on the number of zones treated.

Immediate Aftercare

  • Wear compression garments 24/7 for six weeks, switching to lighter support at week seven.
  • Begin gentle lymphatic drainage within 72 hours to reduce swelling and fibrosis.
  • Walk every few hours while awake to support circulation and reduce clot risk.
  • Avoid beaches, pools, and hot tubs until incisions close and your surgeon clears you.

Possible Side Effects & Precautions

  • Swelling, bruising, and firmness can last several weeks; final contour reveals around month three.
  • Fluid collections (seromas) may occur; we monitor closely and aspirate if needed.
  • Numbness or tingling is common and fades over months as nerves regenerate.
  • Rare complications include contour irregularities or infection; accredited facility standards keep risks low (American Board of Cosmetic Surgery).

Benefits & Results

  • Removes stubborn fat pockets resistant to diet and exercise.
  • Contours waist-to-hip ratio for an hourglass or athletic frame.
  • Improves the fit of clothing and confidence in swimwear.
  • Provides a foundation for further body tightening with Renuvion or wellness programs.

Timeline: Expect noticeable slimming at six weeks, refined definition at three months, and final results by six months.

Candidates & Considerations

Ideal Candidates

  • Clients within 20 to 30 pounds of goal weight seeking targeted contouring.
  • Postpartum or post-weight-loss individuals with good skin elasticity.
  • Those committed to healthy nutrition and movement post-procedure.

Important Considerations

  • Not a weight-loss procedure; we will recommend metabolic support if BMI is above 32.
  • Smoking must cease six weeks before and after to reduce healing complications.
  • Medical conditions like uncontrolled diabetes require optimisation with our wellness team first.

Complementary Treatments

  • Renuvion AbdomenAdd skin tightening for a firm, athletic abdomen.
  • OzempicStabilise weight post-procedure with physician-guided GLP-1 support.
  • Wellness CoachingStay accountable with bespoke nutrition and movement plans.

Caribbean Care Notes

  • Plan recovery in climate-controlled spaces; humidity can intensify swelling in the first two weeks.
  • Schedule garment fittings during cooler morning hours for comfort.

Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can I travel after liposuction 360?

Plan to remain in Kingston for at least seven days for follow-ups and lymphatic care.

When can I return to work?

Desk work usually resumes in 7 to 10 days; more active roles may need three weeks.

Do I have to wear compression?

Yes. Continuous compression is vital for shaping and controlling swelling for six weeks.

Will fat come back?

Removed fat cells do not return, but remaining cells can grow if weight increases, so maintenance is key.
