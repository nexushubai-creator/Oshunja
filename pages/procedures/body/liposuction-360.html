Overview

Liposuction 360 at OSHUNJA provides expert medical care in Kingston, Jamaica.

Our experienced medical team combines advanced techniques with personalized attention.

Each treatment is customized to your unique needs and goals for optimal results.

How It Works & What To Expect

1

Consultation & Mapping

Meet with our fellowship-trained surgeon to discuss your concerns, medical history, and desired outcomes. We'll map your torso individually, considering posture and body shape.

2

Pre-Operative Preparation

Complete labs, arrange caregiver support, and follow pre-surgery nutrition guidelines. We'll provide detailed instructions about medications and what to bring on surgery day.

3

The Procedure

Marked zones are numbed with tumescent anaesthetic that also constricts blood vessels. Micro-cannulas remove fat circumferentially around the torso to sculpt an hourglass or V-taper. Fat is feathered into adjacent zones for smooth transitions.

4

Recovery & Follow-Up

You are placed in a custom compression garment and escorted to a private recovery lounge. Follow-up visits monitor progress at key healing milestones.

During The Procedure

  • Twilight sedation or general anaesthesia depending on the extent of contouring
  • Power-assisted cannulas and vibration technology minimise trauma
  • Incisions are closed with absorbable sutures or left to drain depending on treatment plan
  • Procedure takes 2.5 to 4 hours based on the number of zones treated

Ideal Candidates & Considerations

You May Be an Ideal Candidate If:

  • You're within 20 to 30 pounds of your goal weight seeking targeted contouring
  • You're postpartum or post-weight-loss with good skin elasticity
  • You have stubborn fat deposits that resist diet and exercise
  • You're committed to healthy nutrition and movement post-procedure
  • You're in good overall health with no major medical conditions
  • You have realistic expectations about the procedure and results
  • You're a non-smoker or willing to quit before and after surgery

Important Considerations:

  • Weight: Not a weight-loss procedure; we will recommend metabolic support if BMI is above 32
  • Smoking: Must cease six weeks before and after to reduce healing complications
  • Medical Conditions: Uncontrolled diabetes requires optimisation with our wellness team first
  • Skin Elasticity: Adequate skin elasticity needed for optimal contouring results
  • Recovery Time: Plan to remain in Kingston for at least seven days for follow-ups and lymphatic care
  • Compression Garments: Must commit to wearing compression 24/7 for six weeks
Contraindications: This procedure is not recommended for those with uncontrolled medical conditions, active infections, or certain blood clotting disorders. A thorough medical evaluation will determine your candidacy.

Recovery & Aftercare

First 24-48 Hours

  • Rest in climate-controlled spaces; humidity can intensify swelling
  • Wear compression garments 24/7 as directed
  • Begin gentle lymphatic drainage within 72 hours
  • Walk every few hours while awake to support circulation
  • Take prescribed pain medication as needed

First Week

  • Continue wearing compression garments constantly
  • Attend follow-up appointments as scheduled
  • Desk work usually resumes in 7 to 10 days
  • Avoid beaches, pools, and hot tubs until cleared
  • Light walking is encouraged for circulation

2-6 Weeks

  • Swelling continues to decrease gradually
  • More active roles may resume after three weeks
  • Maintain compression garments for full six weeks
  • Continue lymphatic massage sessions as recommended

6 Weeks to 6 Months

  • Switch to lighter support garments at week seven
  • Gradually resume all normal activities and exercise
  • Final contour reveals around month three
  • Tissues continue settling through month six

Aftercare Instructions

Compression: Wear garments 24/7 for six weeks; vital for shaping and controlling swelling
Activity: Walk regularly but avoid strenuous exercise until cleared by your surgeon
Hydration: Drink plenty of water and reduce salt to control swelling in warm climate
Lymphatic Care: Begin gentle drainage within 72 hours to reduce swelling and fibrosis
Climate: Plan recovery in climate-controlled spaces; schedule garment fittings during cooler morning hours
Follow-up: Attend all scheduled check-ups to monitor healing and progress
When to Contact Us: Call immediately if you experience severe pain, excessive swelling, fever, signs of infection, or fluid collections (seromas).

Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can I travel after liposuction 360?

Plan to remain in Kingston for at least seven days for follow-ups and lymphatic care. This ensures optimal healing and allows our team to monitor your progress during the critical early recovery period.

When can I return to work?

Desk work usually resumes in 7 to 10 days; more active roles may need three weeks. We provide work notes as needed and will guide you based on your specific job requirements and healing progress.

Do I have to wear compression?

Yes. Continuous compression is vital for shaping and controlling swelling for six weeks. The compression garment supports your new contours and significantly improves your final results.

Will fat come back?

Removed fat cells do not return, but remaining cells can grow if weight increases, so maintenance is key. With a stable weight and healthy lifestyle, your results will be long-lasting.

How much fat can be removed safely?

Safety guidelines typically limit removal to 5 liters or about 5% of body weight. Our surgeons prioritize your safety and will determine the appropriate amount based on your anatomy and health status.

Is liposuction 360 painful?

Most patients describe moderate discomfort rather than pain, easily managed with prescribed medication. The tumescent technique used helps minimize post-operative discomfort.

Can this replace diet and exercise?

No, this is not a weight-loss procedure. It's designed for body contouring and removing stubborn fat pockets that resist diet and exercise. Best results come when you're near your goal weight.

What about scarring?

Incisions are tiny (3-5mm) and strategically placed to be inconspicuous. With proper care, they fade to barely visible marks within 6-12 months.

How much does liposuction 360 cost in Jamaica?

The cost varies depending on the extent of treatment and whether it's combined with other procedures. During your consultation, we'll provide a detailed quote. We offer flexible payment plans to make treatment accessible.

Is the procedure safe for dark skin?

Absolutely. Our surgeons have extensive experience treating patients of all skin tones, including melanin-rich Caribbean skin. We use techniques specifically tailored to optimize healing for darker skin types.
