Overview

Vaginoplasty at OSHUNJA surgically tightens the vaginal canal and surrounding muscles to restore firmness and enhance intimate sensation. Our Kingston-based cosmetic gynecology specialists perform this transformative procedure to address vaginal laxity from childbirth, aging, or genetics, helping women reclaim confidence and satisfaction in their intimate lives through expert surgical rejuvenation.

This procedure reconstructs and tightens the vaginal muscles and tissue that may have stretched during childbirth or loosened with age. Vaginoplasty enhances friction during intimacy, improving sensation for both partners while also addressing functional concerns like reduced satisfaction or difficulty with tampon retention. The result is restored vaginal tone that mimics pre-childbirth tightness.

Our board-certified surgeon brings extensive experience in vaginal rejuvenation surgery, combining technical precision with an understanding of female anatomy and intimate wellness. We provide a confidential, judgment-free environment where you can openly discuss your concerns and goals, ensuring complete privacy and compassionate care throughout your journey at our discrete Kingston facility.

How It Works & What To Expect

1

Private Consultation & Assessment

Confidential discussion of your intimate concerns and goals. Pelvic examination to assess vaginal laxity and muscle tone. We explain the surgical approach and create your personalized plan.

2

Pre-Operative Preparation

Pre-surgical instructions including pelvic rest and medication guidelines. Day of surgery preparation in our private suite with anesthesia discussion based on procedure extent.

3

Surgical Tightening

Excess vaginal lining is removed and vaginal muscles are tightened using internal sutures. The vaginal opening may be reduced if desired. Meticulous technique ensures natural feel and optimal function.

4

Recovery & Monitoring

Post-operative monitoring before same-day discharge. Comprehensive aftercare instructions provided including pelvic rest duration, hygiene protocols, and activity restrictions.

5

Follow-Up Care

Scheduled follow-ups to monitor healing and ensure satisfaction. Clearance for resuming intimate activity provided once healing is complete.

Recovery & Aftercare

First Week

  • Rest and avoid strenuous activities
  • Apply ice packs to reduce swelling and discomfort
  • Take prescribed pain medication as needed
  • Maintain strict pelvic rest‚Äîno intercourse, tampons, or douching
  • Keep surgical area clean and dry per instructions

2-3 Weeks

  • Gradually resume light activities as comfort allows
  • Most swelling and discomfort should subside
  • Continue pelvic rest‚Äîno sexual activity
  • Avoid heavy lifting and strenuous exercise
  • Follow-up appointment to monitor healing

4-6 Weeks

  • Resume most normal activities including light exercise
  • Internal healing progressing well
  • Continue avoiding sexual activity until cleared
  • Dissolvable sutures should be absorbed
  • Schedule clearance examination

6-8 Weeks & Beyond

  • Cleared for sexual activity once healing confirmed
  • Resume all normal activities including exercise
  • Final results become apparent as swelling resolves
  • Schedule final follow-up to assess satisfaction

Aftercare Instructions

Hygiene: Gentle cleansing only, pat dry carefully
Pelvic Rest: Strict abstinence from intercourse for 6-8 weeks
Activities: Avoid exercise, heavy lifting for 4-6 weeks
Comfort: Use ice packs and prescribed pain management
Tampons: Avoid tampons for 6-8 weeks
Follow-up: Attend all appointments for clearance
When to Contact Us: Call immediately if you experience severe pain, heavy bleeding, signs of infection (fever, foul-smelling discharge), or difficulty urinating.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does vaginoplasty recovery take?

Initial recovery takes 2-3 weeks. Full recovery with clearance for sexual activity typically takes 6-8 weeks. Complete healing and final results are achieved at 3-6 months.

When can I resume sexual activity?

You must abstain from sexual intercourse for 6-8 weeks post-surgery. We'll provide clearance once healing is confirmed during your follow-up examination. Early resumption can compromise results.

Will vaginoplasty affect future pregnancies?

We recommend completing childbearing before vaginoplasty as vaginal delivery can stretch the repaired muscles and reverse results. The procedure doesn't affect fertility or pregnancy safety.

Is the procedure painful?

Performed under anesthesia, you won't feel pain during surgery. Post-operative discomfort is manageable with prescribed medication and typically subsides within 1-2 weeks.

Will it feel natural to my partner?

Yes. Vaginoplasty restores natural pre-childbirth tightness. Results feel completely natural‚Äînot artificially tight‚Äîenhancing sensation for both partners during intimacy.

Are results permanent?

Yes, results are permanent provided you don't have vaginal deliveries afterward. Proper pelvic floor maintenance and avoiding future vaginal births preserves results long-term.

How much does vaginoplasty cost in Jamaica?

Pricing depends on procedure complexity and whether combining with other surgeries. We provide detailed cost estimates during consultation, including financing options and package pricing.

Can I have a C-section after vaginoplasty?

Yes. If you do have future pregnancies, C-section delivery can preserve your vaginoplasty results. However, we strongly recommend completing childbearing before the procedure.

Is this treatment confidential?

Absolutely. We maintain complete discretion and confidentiality for all intimate procedures. Your privacy is our highest priority from consultation through recovery.

Is the procedure safe for dark skin?

Yes. Our surgeons have extensive experience treating patients of all skin tones, including melanin-rich Caribbean skin. Internal healing is the same regardless of skin tone.
