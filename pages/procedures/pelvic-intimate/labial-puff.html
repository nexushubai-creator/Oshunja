Overview

Labial Puff fat transfer at OSHUNJA uses your own natural fat to restore volume and youthful appearance to the labia majora. Our Kingston-based cosmetic gynecology specialists perform this innovative procedure to address volume loss from aging, childbirth, or genetics, creating natural-looking results that enhance both comfort and confidence.

This advanced fat grafting technique harvests fat from areas like your abdomen or thighs, purifies it, and carefully injects it into the labia majora to restore fullness and smooth wrinkles. Unlike synthetic fillers, using your own fat provides permanent, natural-feeling results with minimal risk of rejection or complications.

Our Kingston clinic provides the highest standard of intimate care in complete privacy. We understand the personal nature of intimate procedures and ensure you feel comfortable, respected, and fully informed throughout your journey from consultation to final results.

How It Works & What To Expect

1

Private Consultation & Planning

Comprehensive consultation to discuss your concerns and goals. We examine the donor and recipient areas, explain the fat transfer process, and create a personalized surgical plan.

2

Fat Harvesting

Using gentle liposuction, we harvest fat from donor areas (typically abdomen, thighs, or flanks). The fat is collected using specialized techniques to preserve cell viability.

3

Fat Purification & Preparation

Harvested fat is processed and purified to remove impurities and damaged cells, concentrating healthy fat cells for optimal survival and natural results.

4

Precise Fat Injection

Using micro-cannulas, purified fat is carefully injected into the labia majora in small amounts at multiple levels to ensure even distribution and natural appearance.

5

Recovery & Follow-up

Post-procedure monitoring and detailed aftercare instructions. Follow-up appointments ensure proper healing and optimal fat survival for lasting results.

Recovery & Aftercare

First 48 Hours

  • Rest and avoid strenuous activities
  • Apply ice packs to reduce swelling at both donor and recipient sites
  • Keep surgical areas clean and dry
  • Take prescribed pain medication as needed
  • Avoid sitting directly on treated areas when possible

First Week

  • Wear compression garments at donor sites as instructed
  • Gentle walking is encouraged to promote healing
  • Avoid heavy lifting, exercise, and prolonged sitting
  • Sleep on your back or side to protect treated areas
  • Follow wound care instructions carefully

2-4 Weeks

  • Gradually resume normal activities as comfort allows
  • Continue wearing compression garments as directed
  • Most swelling and bruising should resolve
  • May resume intimate activities if comfortable
  • Attend follow-up appointments for progress monitoring

6-12 Weeks

  • Resume all normal activities including exercise
  • Final shape becomes more apparent as swelling resolves
  • Fat integration and survival assessment ongoing
  • Schedule follow-up to evaluate results

Aftercare Instructions

Positioning: Avoid prolonged sitting or pressure on treated areas for first 2 weeks
Compression: Wear compression garments at donor sites as prescribed
Activity: Gradually increase activity level as healing progresses
Hygiene: Keep incision sites clean and dry to prevent infection
Follow-up: Attend all scheduled appointments for optimal monitoring
Smoking: Avoid smoking completely to maximize fat survival
When to Contact Us: Call immediately if you experience severe pain, signs of infection (fever, unusual discharge), excessive swelling, or any concerning symptoms.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do labial puff results last?

Results are permanent for the fat that survives the transfer process. Typically, 60-80% of transferred fat remains permanently, providing long-lasting volume enhancement. The survived fat ages naturally with your body.

Is the labial puff procedure painful?

The procedure is performed under anesthesia, so you won't feel pain during surgery. Post-operative discomfort is manageable with prescribed pain medication and typically resolves within a few days to a week.

When will I see final results?

Initial results are visible once swelling subsides (2-4 weeks), but final results appear at 3-6 months when fat survival is complete and tissues have fully integrated.

How much fat survival can I expect?

Typically, 60-80% of transferred fat survives permanently. We may slightly over-correct initially to account for expected fat absorption, ensuring optimal final volume.

Can I combine this with other procedures?

Yes. Labial puff is often combined with labiaplasty, vaginoplasty, or other body contouring procedures. We'll discuss combination options during your consultation.

What are the risks of fat transfer?

Risks are minimal since we use your own fat. Potential complications include infection, asymmetry, over/under-correction, or fat necrosis. Our experienced surgeons minimize these risks through proper technique.

How much does labial puff cost in Jamaica?

Pricing depends on the complexity of your case and areas involved. We provide detailed cost estimates during your consultation, including financing options and package pricing for combined procedures.

Where is the donor fat taken from?

Common donor sites include the abdomen, thighs, flanks, or arms‚Äîareas where you have excess fat. We'll determine the best donor site based on your anatomy and goals during consultation.

When can I resume intimate activities?

Most patients can resume intimate activities after 3-4 weeks, once initial healing is complete. We'll provide specific guidance based on your individual healing progress.

Is the procedure safe for dark skin?

Absolutely. Our surgeons have extensive experience treating patients of all skin tones, including melanin-rich Caribbean skin. We use techniques specifically tailored to optimize healing for darker skin types.
