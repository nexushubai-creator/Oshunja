Overview

Labiaplasty at OSHUNJA surgically reduces enlarged or asymmetrical labia minora (inner vaginal lips) to enhance comfort, eliminate irritation, and improve confidence. Our Kingston-based cosmetic gynecology specialists perform this delicate procedure using advanced techniques that preserve natural appearance, sensation, and function while addressing your specific concerns.

Many women experience physical discomfort from enlarged labia during exercise, intimacy, or when wearing certain clothing. Others seek improvement for aesthetic reasons or to restore symmetry after childbirth. Labiaplasty eliminates chafing, tugging, and self-consciousness by creating proportionate, comfortable anatomy tailored to your body and preferences.

Our board-certified surgeon brings extensive experience in intimate cosmetic procedures, combining surgical precision with an aesthetic eye for natural results. We understand the deeply personal nature of this surgery and ensure complete privacy, compassionate care, and meticulous attention to detail throughout your journey at our discrete Kingston facility.

How It Works & What To Expect

1

Private Consultation & Planning

Confidential discussion of your concerns and goals. Detailed examination to assess labial size, symmetry, and your desired outcome. We explain surgical techniques and create your personalized surgical plan.

2

Pre-Operative Preparation

Pre-surgical instructions provided including medication guidelines. Day of surgery, you're prepared in our comfortable private suite with anesthesia options discussed based on your preference and procedure complexity.

3

Surgical Procedure

Using either trim method (edge removal) or wedge technique (V-shaped removal), excess labial tissue is precisely removed. Meticulous suturing with dissolvable stitches ensures minimal scarring and natural appearance.

4

Recovery & Aftercare

You're monitored post-operatively before discharge the same day. Comprehensive aftercare instructions provided including pain management, hygiene protocols, and activity restrictions for optimal healing.

5

Follow-Up Care

Scheduled follow-up appointments to monitor healing, remove any non-dissolvable sutures if used, and ensure you're completely satisfied with your results.

Ideal Candidates & Considerations

Labiaplasty May Be Right for You If:

  • You experience physical discomfort from enlarged labia during exercise, cycling, intimacy, or when wearing tight clothing—this surgery eliminates irritation and chafing
  • You notice asymmetry between your labia that affects your confidence or causes aesthetic concerns—we create balanced, proportionate appearance
  • You feel self-conscious about labial appearance during intimate moments—restored confidence enhances quality of life
  • You've completed childbearing or willing to wait until after future pregnancies—pregnancy can affect results
  • You're in good overall health without active infections in the treatment area
  • You have realistic expectations about surgical outcomes and understand the recovery process
  • You value privacy and discretion in addressing intimate concerns—our Kingston facility ensures complete confidentiality

Important Safety Considerations:

  • Pregnancy & Nursing: Surgery should be delayed until after completion of childbearing and nursing to ensure lasting results
  • Active Infections: Any vaginal infections or inflammation must be fully resolved before surgery
  • Realistic Expectations: Results are natural-looking—not dramatic transformation but proportionate, comfortable anatomy
  • Healing Process: Full recovery takes 6-8 weeks with activity restrictions—plan accordingly for work and lifestyle
  • Scarring: While minimal, some scarring is inevitable—our techniques minimize visibility
  • Sensation: Temporary numbness may occur but sensation typically returns fully within months
Contraindications: Not recommended for those with active infections, uncontrolled bleeding disorders, pregnancy, unrealistic expectations, or psychological conditions requiring counseling first.

Recovery & Aftercare

First 48 Hours

  • Rest and avoid strenuous activities
  • Apply ice packs to reduce swelling
  • Keep surgical area clean and dry
  • Take prescribed pain medication as needed
  • Avoid sitting for prolonged periods when possible

First Week

  • Continue gentle care of surgical site
  • Wear loose, comfortable clothing
  • Avoid heavy lifting, exercise, and sexual activity
  • Follow wound care instructions carefully
  • Swelling and bruising are normal and will subside

2-4 Weeks

  • Gradually resume light activities as comfort allows
  • Most swelling should resolve
  • Continue avoiding strenuous exercise and sexual activity
  • Attend follow-up appointments for progress monitoring
  • Dissolvable sutures will begin to dissolve

6-8 Weeks

  • Resume all normal activities including exercise
  • Sexual activity can gradually resume (with clearance)
  • Final results become more apparent
  • Minimal residual swelling
  • Schedule final follow-up to assess results

Aftercare Instructions

Hygiene: Keep area clean with gentle cleansing, pat dry carefully
Clothing: Wear loose cotton underwear and avoid tight pants
Activities: Avoid exercise, heavy lifting for 6 weeks
Intimacy: Wait 6-8 weeks before resuming sexual activity
Comfort: Use ice packs and prescribed pain management
Follow-up: Attend all scheduled appointments for monitoring
When to Contact Us: Call immediately if you experience severe pain, signs of infection (fever, unusual discharge, foul odor), excessive bleeding, or any concerning symptoms.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does labiaplasty recovery take?

Initial recovery takes 1-2 weeks with most swelling subsiding. Full recovery and return to all activities including sexual activity typically takes 6-8 weeks. Final results are visible at 3-6 months once all healing is complete.

Is labiaplasty painful?

The procedure is performed under anesthesia so you won't feel pain during surgery. Post-operative discomfort is manageable with prescribed pain medication and typically subsides within the first week.

Will there be visible scarring?

Our advanced techniques minimize scarring. Incisions are placed along natural edges and heal with minimal visibility. Any residual scarring typically fades significantly over 6-12 months and is not noticeable.

Will sensation be affected?

Modern labiaplasty techniques preserve nerve pathways. Temporary numbness may occur but normal sensation typically returns within 3-6 months. Sexual function and pleasure are not negatively affected.

Can I have children after labiaplasty?

Yes. Labiaplasty doesn't affect fertility or ability to have vaginal delivery. However, we recommend completing childbearing first as pregnancy and delivery can affect results.

What's the difference between trim and wedge technique?

Trim method removes the edge of excess tissue, while wedge technique removes a V-shaped section preserving the natural edge. Your surgeon will recommend the best approach based on your anatomy and goals.

How much does labiaplasty cost in Jamaica?

Pricing depends on technique used and whether combining with other procedures. We provide detailed cost estimates during your consultation, including financing options and package pricing.

When can I return to work?

Most patients return to desk work within 3-5 days. Physical jobs may require 1-2 weeks off. We'll provide personalized guidance based on your occupation and procedure specifics.

Is this treatment confidential?

Absolutely. We maintain complete discretion and confidentiality for all intimate procedures. Your privacy is our highest priority from consultation through recovery and follow-up care.

Is the procedure safe for dark skin?

Yes. Our surgeons have extensive experience treating patients of all skin tones, including melanin-rich Caribbean skin. We use techniques specifically tailored to optimize healing for darker skin types.
