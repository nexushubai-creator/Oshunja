Overview

Hymenoplasty at OSHUNJA is a delicate surgical procedure that reconstructs the hymenal tissue for personal, cultural, religious, or psychological reasons. Our Kingston-based cosmetic gynecology specialists provide this highly confidential service with complete discretion, compassion, and zero judgment. We understand the sensitive nature of this procedure and ensure absolute privacy throughout your entire journey, from consultation to recovery.

The hymen is a thin membrane at the vaginal opening that can be torn or stretched through various activities including first intercourse, sports, tampon use, or injury. Hymenoplasty surgically restores the hymenal ring by rejoining torn tissue, creating natural appearance and function. The procedure is performed with meticulous precision to achieve results that are anatomically correct and virtually undetectable.

Our board-certified surgeon brings extensive experience in intimate reconstructive procedures, combining surgical expertise with deep cultural sensitivity and understanding. We provide a safe, supportive environment where you can discuss your needs openly without fear of judgment. Your confidentiality is our highest priority, and we maintain complete discretion throughout your care at our private Kingston facility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is hymenoplasty completely confidential?

Absolutely. We maintain the highest level of privacy and discretion. All consultations, medical records, and appointments are strictly confidential. Your privacy is our paramount concern.

How long does recovery take?

Initial healing takes 1-2 weeks. Complete healing requires 6-8 weeks with strict pelvic rest. Most daily activities can resume within 2-3 weeks, but intercourse must be avoided for full healing period.

Will the results look natural?

Yes. Our surgeon uses meticulous technique to create results that are virtually undetectable and anatomically natural. The reconstruction appears and functions like intact hymenal tissue.

Is the procedure painful?

Performed under anesthesia, you won't feel pain during surgery. Post-operative discomfort is minimal and manageable with prescribed medication. Most patients report mild discomfort that resolves within days.

How long do results last?

The reconstruction is permanent until intercourse occurs. The reconstructed hymen will tear with penetration, similar to natural hymenal tissue. Results maintain natural appearance until that time.

Can this be detected by examination?

When performed by an expert surgeon, hymenoplasty creates results that are virtually undetectable. The reconstruction appears natural and anatomically correct.

Do I need to explain why I want this procedure?

No. We respect that this is your personal decision. You don't need to justify or explain your reasons. We provide judgment-free care focused solely on your health and desired outcome.

How much does hymenoplasty cost in Jamaica?

Pricing is discussed during your confidential consultation. We provide transparent cost estimates and offer discreet payment options. Financing may be available.

Can I have this done before marriage?

Yes. Many patients choose hymenoplasty before marriage for personal, cultural, or religious reasons. We ensure complete healing is achieved well before your planned date with appropriate timing.

Is the procedure safe for dark skin?

Yes. Our surgeons have extensive experience with all skin tones, including melanin-rich Caribbean skin. Healing is the same regardless of complexion with proper surgical technique.
