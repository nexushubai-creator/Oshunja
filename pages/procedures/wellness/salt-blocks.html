Overview

Halotherapy (salt therapy) at OSHUNJA uses pharmaceutical-grade salt particles dispersed throughout our dedicated salt room to support respiratory health, improve skin conditions, boost immunity, and promote deep relaxation. This natural therapy involves breathing air saturated with microscopic salt particles that have antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, and mucolytic (mucus-clearing) properties proven effective for asthma, allergies, bronchitis, COPD, eczema, psoriasis, and overall wellness.

In Kingston's tropical environment where humidity and allergens can challenge respiratory health, our climate-controlled salt therapy room provides a tranquil escape. Sessions last 45-60 minutes in a serene environment designed to mimic natural salt caves. As you relax in zero-gravity chairs, a halogenerator grinds pharmaceutical-grade salt into microscopic particles (1-5 microns) and disperses them throughout the room, where they penetrate deep into your respiratory system and settle on your skin.

The therapy is completely passive‚Äîsimply breathe normally while relaxing, reading, or meditating. Salt particles work their way into sinuses, bronchial tubes, and lungs, reducing inflammation, clearing mucus, killing bacteria, and improving oxygen exchange. Many clients with chronic respiratory or skin conditions experience significant relief with regular sessions, while healthy individuals enjoy immune support, stress reduction, and enhanced well-being.

Arrive in comfortable clothing (white or light colors recommended as salt may leave residue). Remove shoes before entering the salt room. Our staff explains the session, answers questions, and ensures you're comfortable with the environment and process.

Enter our tranquil salt therapy room featuring walls and floors covered in Himalayan salt, creating a cave-like microclimate. Recline in comfortable zero-gravity chairs. Calming music, dim lighting, and serene ambiance promote immediate relaxation.

3

Salt Dispersion (45-60 minutes)

Our halogenerator grinds pharmaceutical-grade salt into microscopic particles (1-5 microns) and disperses them into the air. Simply breathe normally‚Äîno special technique needed. Salt particles penetrate deep into sinuses, bronchial passages, and lungs while settling on skin. You may notice slight salty taste or tingle on skin‚Äîthis is normal and beneficial.

4

Relaxation & Therapeutic Action

Relax, meditate, read, or simply rest as salt works its magic. The antibacterial, anti-inflammatory properties reduce respiratory inflammation, clear mucus, open airways, and kill pathogens. Skin benefits from salt's natural antiseptic and pH-balancing effects. Many clients experience immediate easier breathing and sense of calm.

5

After your session, you may experience increased mucus production for 12-24 hours‚Äîthis is positive, showing salt is clearing congestion. Benefits continue for hours post-session. For chronic conditions, 10-15 sessions (2-3x weekly) recommended initially. Maintenance sessions monthly sustain benefits.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does salt therapy help respiratory conditions?

Microscopic salt particles have antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, and mucolytic (mucus-thinning) properties. When inhaled, they penetrate deep into airways, reducing inflammation, killing pathogens, clearing mucus, and opening airways. This provides natural relief for asthma, allergies, sinusitis, bronchitis, and COPD.

How many sessions will I need?

For acute conditions (cold, sinus infection), 3-5 sessions may suffice. Chronic conditions (asthma, COPD, eczema) benefit from 10-15 initial sessions (2-3x weekly) followed by monthly maintenance. Wellness and prevention: 1-2 sessions monthly. Benefits are cumulative‚Äîconsistency matters.

Is salt therapy safe for children?

Yes! Halotherapy is excellent for children 2+ years old with asthma, allergies, frequent colds, or eczema. It's completely natural, non-invasive, and drug-free. Many parents report significant reduction in children's respiratory symptoms and medication needs with regular salt therapy sessions.

Can I do salt therapy if I'm pregnant?

Yes. Unlike many therapies, halotherapy is safe during pregnancy and can actually help with pregnancy-related congestion, sinus pressure, and stress. The natural, drug-free approach makes it ideal for expectant mothers seeking respiratory relief without medications.

Will salt therapy help my skin condition?

Yes. Salt has natural anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and pH-balancing properties beneficial for eczema, psoriasis, acne, and dermatitis. Salt particles settle on skin during sessions, reducing inflammation and promoting healing. Consistent sessions (2-3x weekly initially) show best results for chronic skin conditions.

Why do I cough more after salt therapy?

Increased coughing and mucus production for 24-48 hours post-session is a positive sign‚Äîsalt is clearing accumulated mucus and debris from your airways. This "clearing response" is temporary and beneficial. Stay hydrated to support the process. If it persists beyond 48 hours, contact us.

Can salt therapy replace my asthma medications?

No. Halotherapy is complementary therapy that supports medical treatment but doesn't replace it. Many clients reduce medication needs over time with doctor's guidance, but never stop prescribed medications without physician approval. Salt therapy enhances conventional treatment effectiveness.

What should I wear to a salt therapy session?

Wear comfortable, light-colored clothing (white or pastels)‚Äîsalt particles may leave white residue on dark fabrics. Bring socks as you'll remove shoes. Avoid perfumes or strong scents which can irritate sensitive respiratory systems. Come with clean, dry skin for maximum benefit.

Is halotherapy scientifically proven?

Yes. Salt therapy has been used in Eastern Europe for centuries and is backed by numerous clinical studies showing efficacy for respiratory and skin conditions. Research demonstrates significant improvements in asthma control, reduced inflammation, improved lung function, and symptom relief for various conditions.

Can I bring my phone or read during sessions?

Yes. Salt therapy is passive‚Äîyou simply breathe normally while relaxing. Many clients read, meditate, listen to music, or use their phones. However, we encourage disconnecting to maximize relaxation benefits. The tranquil environment is perfect for mindfulness and stress reduction.
