Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an IV therapy session take?

Most infusions run 30-60 minutes depending on formula. Total visit time including setup is about 60-90 minutes. Relax in our comfortable Kingston lounge.

Does IV therapy hurt?

Brief pinch during needle insertion, then no pain. Some formulas cause temporary cool sensation as they enter your vein. Most patients find it very comfortable.

How quickly will I feel results?

Many people feel energized immediately or within hours. Full benefits develop over 24-48 hours as nutrients reach tissues. Effects typically last days to weeks.

How often should I get IV therapy?

Depends on goals. Athletic recovery: weekly. Immune support during illness: 2-3x/week. General wellness: monthly. We'll recommend ideal frequency for you.

Is IV therapy safe?

Very safe when administered by trained professionals. We use sterile technique, pharmaceutical-grade vitamins, and screen for contraindications. Side effects are rare and mild.
