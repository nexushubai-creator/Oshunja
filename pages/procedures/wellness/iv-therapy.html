Overview

IV vitamin therapy at OSHUNJA delivers essential nutrients, vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants directly into your bloodstream for 100% absorption in Kingston, Jamaica. Whether you need an energy boost, immune support, athletic recovery, hangover relief, or beauty enhancement, our customized IV formulas provide rapid, effective results that oral supplements simply can't match.

Jamaica's demanding climate and active lifestyle can deplete your body's nutrient stores. IV therapy rapidly replenishes hydration, electrolytes, and vital nutrients—bypassing the digestive system for immediate cellular delivery. Feel the difference within minutes as vitamins flow directly where your body needs them most.

From high-dose vitamin C for immune support to NAD+ for anti-aging and energy, glutathione for detox and skin brightening, to customized athletic recovery formulas—we offer targeted IV cocktails for every wellness goal. Relax in our comfortable Kingston lounge while we restore your vitality from the inside out.

How It Works & What To Expect

1

Health Assessment & Consultation

Begin with a brief health screening and consultation. We review your wellness goals, current symptoms (fatigue, dehydration, illness recovery, etc.), medical history, medications, and any allergies. This ensures we select the safest, most effective IV formula for your specific needs and identify any contraindications.

2

Personalized Formula Selection

Based on your assessment, we recommend the optimal IV cocktail from our menu: Myers' Cocktail for general wellness, Immune Boost for cold/flu defense, Athlete Recovery for sports performance, Beauty Glow for skin brightening, Hangover Relief for rapid rehydration, or NAD+ for anti-aging and energy. Custom formulas can also be created to address your unique requirements.

3

IV Access & Setup

Get comfortable in our relaxing Kingston IV lounge. Our trained medical professional cleanses your arm with antiseptic, applies a small butterfly needle or IV catheter (brief pinch), and secures it with medical tape. The IV line connects to your customized vitamin bag. The entire setup takes just 2-3 minutes.

4

Infusion Session (30-60 Minutes)

Relax while your IV formula drips directly into your bloodstream for 100% absorption. Many clients describe a cool sensation as vitamins enter the vein—completely normal. Bring a book, catch up on emails via WiFi, watch TV, listen to music, or simply close your eyes and rest. We monitor you throughout and adjust flow rate for your comfort.

5

Completion & Aftercare

Once your infusion is complete, we remove the IV (no pain), apply a small bandage, and have you rest briefly before standing. You're free to resume all normal activities immediately—drive, work, exercise. Most clients feel energized, hydrated, and refreshed right away, with continued improvement over the next 24-48 hours.

6

Follow-Up & Maintenance

Based on your goals and response, we'll recommend an optimal frequency for follow-up sessions: weekly for athletic training or acute illness recovery, bi-weekly for sustained energy and wellness, or monthly for general maintenance. Regular IV therapy maintains optimal nutrient levels and supports long-term vitality.

Important Considerations:

  • Medical History: Disclose kidney disease, heart conditions, or electrolyte disorders—some formulas may not be appropriate

  • Cost: IV therapy is typically out-of-pocket. We'll discuss pricing upfront

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an IV therapy session take?

Most infusions run 30-60 minutes depending on the formula and your tolerance. High-dose vitamin C or NAD+ infusions may take longer (60-90 minutes). Total visit time including health screening, IV setup, infusion, and brief post-treatment observation is typically 60-90 minutes. Plan accordingly and bring something to read or work on.

Does IV therapy hurt?

There's a brief pinch during needle insertion (similar to getting blood drawn), then no pain during the infusion itself. Some formulas, especially those with high magnesium or cold fluids, can cause a temporary cool sensation or mild arm tingling as they enter your vein—completely normal and not painful. Most patients find IV therapy very comfortable and even relaxing.

How quickly will I feel results from IV therapy?

Many people feel energized, refreshed, and more alert immediately or within 1-2 hours of finishing their infusion. Full benefits develop over the next 24-48 hours as nutrients reach tissues and cells. Effects typically last anywhere from several days to 2-3 weeks depending on the formula, your metabolism, and your body's nutrient demands. Regular sessions maintain optimal levels.

How often should I get IV therapy?

Frequency depends on your goals: Athletic recovery: weekly during training; Immune support during illness: 2-3 times per week until recovered; Beauty/anti-aging: weekly for 4-6 weeks, then monthly maintenance; General wellness: monthly or as needed; NAD+ therapy: weekly for intensive protocol, then monthly maintenance. We'll recommend an optimal schedule based on your specific needs and response.

Is IV therapy safe?

Very safe when administered by trained medical professionals using sterile technique and pharmaceutical-grade ingredients. We screen for contraindications (kidney disease, heart conditions, electrolyte imbalances), use proper protocols, and monitor you throughout. Side effects are rare and typically mild: bruising at insertion site, cool sensation during infusion, or temporary mineral taste. Serious adverse events are extremely uncommon with proper screening and administration.

What's in a Myers' Cocktail and why is it so popular?

The Myers' Cocktail contains vitamin C, B-complex vitamins (B1, B2, B3, B5, B6), B12, magnesium, and calcium. It's the most popular IV formula because it addresses multiple wellness concerns simultaneously: fatigue, immune weakness, stress, muscle aches, and general depletion. Originally developed by Dr. John Myers in the 1960s, it remains the gold standard for IV wellness therapy and has helped countless patients with chronic fatigue, fibromyalgia, migraines, and general health optimization.

Can IV therapy really cure a hangover?

Yes! Our Hangover Relief IV is remarkably effective. It rapidly rehydrates with 1 liter of fluids, replenishes electrolytes (sodium, potassium, magnesium), delivers anti-nausea medication to settle your stomach, provides pain relief for headaches, and restores B-vitamins and other nutrients depleted by alcohol metabolism. Most clients report feeling dramatically better within 30-60 minutes—far more effective than oral remedies that must pass through your already upset stomach.

What is glutathione and why is it so popular for beauty?

Glutathione is the body's "master antioxidant"—a powerful tripeptide that detoxifies cells, neutralizes free radicals, brightens skin, reduces hyperpigmentation and melasma, supports anti-aging, and promotes overall cellular health. It's extremely popular for beauty benefits because it can lighten dark spots, even skin tone, and create a radiant glow. We offer glutathione as an IV "push" (rapid 5-10 minute injection) or added to longer infusions. Many clients get it weekly for skin brightening.

Is IV therapy better than taking oral vitamins and supplements?

For rapid, noticeable results, yes. Oral supplements must pass through your digestive system where absorption rates are limited to 20-50% due to stomach acid, digestive enzymes, and first-pass liver metabolism. IV therapy delivers 100% bioavailability directly to your bloodstream, bypassing digestion entirely. Nutrients reach your cells immediately at therapeutic doses impossible to achieve orally. This produces faster, more dramatic effects. However, oral supplements are still valuable for daily maintenance between IV sessions.

Can I get IV therapy if I'm afraid of needles?

Yes! Many needle-phobic clients successfully receive IV therapy. We use very small butterfly needles (much smaller than blood donation needles), can apply topical numbing cream before insertion, and provide distraction techniques during the quick stick. Once the IV is in place, there's zero pain—just relax for 30-60 minutes while nutrients infuse. Most anxious clients are pleasantly surprised by how easy and comfortable the experience is. The dramatic benefits make the brief initial discomfort worthwhile.

What's the difference between an IV drip and an IV push?

An IV drip delivers nutrients slowly over 30-60 minutes through a hanging bag—ideal for larger volumes of fluids and vitamins like Myers' Cocktail or hydration therapy. An IV push is a concentrated dose delivered rapidly via syringe over 3-10 minutes—commonly used for glutathione, high-dose vitamin C boosts, or quick energy shots. Pushes are faster but more concentrated; drips are gentler and allow for more fluid rehydration.

Can I exercise or drink alcohol after IV therapy?

Exercise: Wait at least 2-4 hours after IV therapy before intense workouts to allow nutrients to circulate and avoid dizziness. Light activity is fine immediately. Alcohol: Best to avoid alcohol for at least 24 hours after IV therapy—especially if you got an immune or detox formula. If you received a Hangover Relief IV, you technically can drink again, but please give your body a break to recover properly!

Will insurance cover IV therapy?

Most insurance plans do not cover elective IV vitamin therapy for wellness, beauty, or performance enhancement as it's considered preventive/elective rather than medically necessary treatment. However, IV therapy for specific medical conditions (severe dehydration, documented vitamin deficiencies, certain chronic illnesses) may be covered with proper documentation. We recommend checking with your insurance provider. Self-pay pricing is transparent and provided upfront.
