Who Benefits from Dietetics?

You May Benefit from Dietetics If:

  • You have diabetes, prediabetes, or metabolic syndrome requiring dietary management
  • You're managing hypertension, high cholesterol, or cardiovascular disease
  • You need weight management support‚Äîwhether losing, gaining, or maintaining
  • You have digestive issues, food intolerances, or inflammatory conditions
  • You're an athlete seeking performance nutrition optimization
  • You're preparing for or recovering from bariatric or other surgery
  • You want to improve overall health, energy, and wellbeing through better nutrition

What to Expect:

  • Comprehensive Assessment: Detailed review of medical history, current diet, lab results, medications, and lifestyle factors
  • Realistic Goals: We set achievable nutrition targets that fit your life‚Äîno impossible standards or extreme restrictions
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Plans incorporate foods you actually enjoy, including traditional Jamaican dishes and local ingredients
  • Gradual Changes: Sustainable habit formation rather than drastic overnight transformations
  • Ongoing Adjustments: Regular follow-ups allow us to refine your plan as needs and circumstances evolve
  • Collaborative Care: We coordinate with your physician and other providers for integrated treatment

Ongoing Support & Follow-Up

Nutrition counseling is most effective with regular follow-up. After your initial consultation, we typically schedule:

Initial Phase (Weeks 1-4)

Weekly or bi-weekly check-ins to establish new habits, troubleshoot challenges, and make early adjustments to your meal plan.

Adjustment Phase (Months 2-3)

Monthly sessions to monitor progress, review lab results if applicable, and refine strategies based on what's working.

Maintenance Phase (Ongoing)

Quarterly check-ins to maintain accountability, address new challenges, and ensure long-term success.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral to see the dietitian?

No referral needed! You can book directly. However, if your insurance covers nutrition counseling, they may require a physician referral‚Äîwe can help coordinate this.

How many sessions will I need?

Most patients benefit from an initial consultation plus 3-6 follow-up sessions over 3-6 months. Ongoing maintenance visits help sustain long-term success.

Can you accommodate cultural/religious dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating plans that honor your cultural preferences, religious requirements, and food traditions including Caribbean cuisine.

Will I have to give up foods I love?

No! We focus on balance and moderation rather than elimination. We'll help you enjoy your favorite foods while meeting your health goals.

Do you provide meal plans?

Yes, personalized meal plans with specific recipes, portions, and timing tailored to your schedule, preferences, budget, and nutritional needs.
