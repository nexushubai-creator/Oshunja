Overview

The Clitoral PRP Enhancement (commonly called the "Clit Shot") at OSHUNJA uses your body's own platelet-rich plasma to enhance clitoral sensitivity and sexual response. Our Kingston-based sexual wellness specialists deliver this innovative treatment in complete privacy, helping women reclaim confidence in their intimate lives through natural regenerative medicine.

This non-surgical procedure harnesses the healing power of your own blood platelets, concentrating growth factors that stimulate tissue regeneration and increase sensitivity. Unlike synthetic treatments, PRP uses your body's natural healing mechanisms to enhance intimate wellness and improve sexual satisfaction.

Our team understands that sexual wellness is deeply personal. We provide a judgment-free environment where you can openly discuss concerns and goals. Each treatment is customized to your unique anatomy, needs, and desired outcomes, ensuring both comfort and optimal results in our discrete Kingston facility.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do Clitoral PRP results last?

Results typically last 6-12 months, with many patients experiencing sustained improvement. Individual results vary based on age, overall health, and lifestyle factors. Maintenance treatments can help extend benefits.

Is the Clit Shot painful?

Most patients report minimal discomfort. We use topical numbing cream and ultra-fine needles to ensure comfort. Any mild discomfort during injection is brief and well-tolerated.

When will I notice results?

Initial improvements typically appear within 4-6 weeks as tissue regeneration occurs. Full benefits are usually achieved by 8-12 weeks, with continued improvement possible over time.

Is this treatment safe and natural?

Yes. Clitoral PRP uses your own blood platelets, making it completely natural with minimal risk of allergic reaction or rejection. The procedure has an excellent safety profile when performed by qualified providers.

Can I return to intimate activities immediately?

We recommend waiting 24-48 hours before resuming intimate contact to allow initial healing. After this brief period, normal intimate activities can be gradually resumed.

Are there side effects?

Side effects are minimal and temporary, potentially including mild swelling, tenderness, or slight bruising at injection sites. These typically resolve within 24-48 hours.

How much does Clitoral PRP cost in Jamaica?

Pricing is discussed during your private consultation based on your individual needs. We offer financing options and package pricing for combination treatments. Contact us for specific pricing information.

How is this different from the O-Shot?

While the O-Shot targets the vaginal area for overall sexual function, Clitoral PRP specifically focuses on enhancing clitoral sensitivity. They can be combined for comprehensive intimate wellness.

Is this treatment confidential?

Absolutely. We maintain complete discretion and confidentiality for all intimate wellness treatments. Your privacy is our highest priority, from consultation through follow-up care.

Is the procedure safe for dark skin?

Yes. Our physicians have extensive experience treating patients of all skin tones, including melanin-rich Caribbean skin. PRP therapy works safely and effectively for all skin types.
