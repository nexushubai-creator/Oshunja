How It Works & What To Expect

1

Private Consultation

Confidential discussion of your concerns and goals. We review your medical history, explain the P-Shot protocol, address erectile dysfunction or enhancement goals, and answer all questions privately.

2

Blood Draw & PRP Preparation

A small blood sample is drawn and processed in our centrifuge to concentrate platelets. This creates potent PRP containing growth factors and healing proteins.

3

Numbing Application

Topical anesthetic cream and/or penile block is applied ensuring complete comfort. We wait for full numbing before proceeding with injections.

4

Precise PRP Injection

Using ultra-fine needles, PRP is precisely injected into the shaft and glans of the penis following the patented P-Shot protocol. Strategic placement maximizes blood flow, sensitivity, and tissue growth.

5

Vacuum Pump Application

Immediately after injection, a vacuum pump is applied for 10 minutes to enhance PRP distribution and results. You'll be provided a pump for at-home use to optimize outcomes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do P-Shot results last?

Results typically last 12-18 months, with many men experiencing sustained improvement. Individual results vary based on age, overall health, and lifestyle factors. Maintenance treatments can extend benefits.

Is the P-Shot painful?

Most men report minimal discomfort. We use comprehensive numbing including topical cream and/or penile block to ensure comfort. Any mild discomfort during injection is brief and well-tolerated.

When will I see results?

Initial improvements typically appear within 1-2 weeks. Optimal results develop over 8-12 weeks as tissue regeneration occurs. Size increases are gradual and natural-looking.

How much size increase can I expect?

Most men experience 10-20% increase in girth and up to 1 inch in length. Results vary by individual and depend on following the vacuum pump protocol post-procedure.

Does it help with erectile dysfunction?

Yes. The P-Shot significantly improves erectile function by enhancing blood flow and tissue health. Many men reduce or eliminate their need for ED medications after treatment.

Is this treatment safe and natural?

Completely. The P-Shot uses your own blood platelets, making it natural with minimal risk of side effects. The procedure has an excellent safety profile when performed by qualified providers.

Can I combine this with other treatments?

Absolutely. The P-Shot works exceptionally well with shockwave therapy, testosterone optimization, and other men's wellness treatments for comprehensive enhancement.

Is this treatment confidential?

Completely. We maintain absolute discretion and confidentiality for all men's health treatments. Your privacy is our highest priority, from consultation through follow-up care.

Is the procedure safe for all men?

Yes. Our physicians have extensive experience treating men of all backgrounds. PRP therapy works safely and effectively regardless of age or ethnicity when proper protocols are followed.
