Overview

The O-Shot¬Æ (Orgasm Shot) at OSHUNJA uses your body's own platelet-rich plasma to rejuvenate vaginal tissue, enhance sexual function, and improve intimate wellness. Our Kingston-based sexual wellness specialists deliver this innovative treatment using the patented O-Shot protocol, helping women reclaim confidence and satisfaction in their intimate lives through natural regenerative medicine.

This non-surgical procedure harnesses the healing power of your own blood platelets, concentrating growth factors that stimulate tissue regeneration, increase sensitivity, and improve blood flow to intimate areas. The O-Shot can address multiple concerns including decreased libido, difficulty achieving orgasm, vaginal dryness, and stress urinary incontinence‚Äîall without hormones or synthetic materials.

How It Works & What To Expect

1

Private Consultation

Confidential discussion of your intimate concerns and wellness goals. We review your medical history, explain the O-Shot protocol, and answer all questions in a comfortable, judgment-free environment.

2

Blood Draw & PRP Preparation

A small blood sample is drawn and processed in our centrifuge to concentrate platelets. This creates nutrient-rich PRP containing your body's natural growth factors and healing proteins.

3

Numbing Application

Topical anesthetic cream is applied to treatment areas ensuring maximum comfort. We wait for complete numbing before proceeding with injections.

4

Precise PRP Injection

Using ultra-fine needles, PRP is precisely injected into specific areas: the clitoral area (C-spot) and the upper vaginal wall (G-spot area) following the patented O-Shot protocol for optimal results.

5

Immediate Recovery

No downtime required. You receive detailed aftercare instructions and can resume normal activities immediately, with intimate contact resumed after 24-48 hours for optimal healing.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do O-Shot results last?

Results typically last 6-12 months, with many patients experiencing sustained improvement. Individual results vary based on age, overall health, and lifestyle factors. Maintenance treatments can extend benefits.

Is the O-Shot painful?

Most patients report minimal discomfort. We use topical numbing cream and ultra-fine needles to ensure comfort. Any mild discomfort during injection is brief and well-tolerated.

When will I notice results?

Initial improvements typically appear within 1-2 weeks as tissue regeneration begins. Full benefits are usually achieved by 8-12 weeks, with continued improvement possible over time.

Is the O-Shot safe and natural?

Yes. The O-Shot uses your own blood platelets, making it completely natural with minimal risk of allergic reaction or rejection. The procedure has an excellent safety profile when performed by qualified providers.

Can I return to intimate activities immediately?

We recommend waiting 24-48 hours before resuming intimate contact to allow initial healing. After this brief period, normal intimate activities can be gradually resumed.

Does it help with urinary incontinence?

Yes. Many patients experience significant improvement in stress urinary incontinence. The PRP strengthens urethral tissue and improves bladder control, reducing leaking during physical activities.

How much does the O-Shot cost in Jamaica?

Pricing is discussed during your private consultation based on your individual needs. We offer financing options and package pricing for combination treatments. Contact us for specific pricing information.

Can it help with vaginal dryness?

Absolutely. The O-Shot stimulates natural lubrication by rejuvenating vaginal tissue and improving blood flow. Many patients notice significant improvement in moisture and comfort during intimacy.

Is this treatment confidential?

Completely. We maintain absolute discretion and confidentiality for all intimate wellness treatments. Your privacy is our highest priority, from consultation through follow-up care.

Is the procedure safe for dark skin?

Yes. Our physicians have extensive experience treating patients of all skin tones, including melanin-rich Caribbean skin. PRP therapy works safely and effectively for all skin types.
