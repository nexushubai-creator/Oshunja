Overview

Menopause is a natural transition—but symptoms like hot flashes, night sweats, sleep disruption, mood shifts, brain fog, weight changes, and vaginal dryness can significantly impact daily life. At OSHUNJA, we provide comprehensive, personalized care to help you feel like yourself again.

Your plan may include lifestyle strategies, non‑hormonal options, and bioidentical hormone therapy (BHRT) when appropriate. We prioritize safety and evidence‑based dosing, tailoring treatment to your symptoms, medical history, and preferences.

For intimate wellness, we can address vaginal dryness, discomfort, and pelvic floor changes with options like O‑Shot (vaginal PRP) and Emsella—offered in a confidential, compassionate setting in Kingston.

We review your symptoms, history, cycle changes, medication use, and goals. A confidential space to discuss everything affecting your well‑being.

5

Follow‑Up & Optimization

We check in regularly and adjust dosing or strategies as needed for sustained relief and safety.

Ideal Candidates & Considerations

Menopause Management May Be Right For You If:

  • You’re experiencing hot flashes, night sweats, or sleep disruption
  • You’ve noticed mood changes, brain fog, or reduced focus
  • You’re dealing with vaginal dryness, discomfort, or reduced libido
  • You want personalized options—hormonal and non‑hormonal
  • You value close follow‑up and clear safety guidance

Important Safety Considerations:

  • Contraindications: History of estrogen‑dependent cancers, active liver disease, or unexplained vaginal bleeding
  • Route matters: Transdermal estrogen may carry lower clot risk than oral in some cases
  • Monitoring: We recommend periodic check‑ins and targeted labs to ensure safety
  • Preferences: Non‑hormonal paths are available and effective for many symptoms
Note: We coordinate with your physicians as needed to personalize care based on your history and goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need hormones to manage menopause?

Not always. Many symptoms can be managed with lifestyle and non‑hormonal options. When BHRT is appropriate, we individualize dosing and monitoring.

Are bioidentical hormones safe?

When appropriately prescribed and monitored, BHRT can be safe and effective for many women. We discuss benefits, risks, and the safest routes for you.

How soon will I feel better?

Many women notice improvements within 2–4 weeks; comprehensive benefits typically develop over 6–12 weeks as your plan is optimized.

Can you help with vaginal dryness and discomfort?

Yes—options include local vaginal estrogen, O‑Shot (PRP), and pelvic floor therapies like Emsella for comprehensive intimate wellness.

What if I can’t or don’t want hormones?

We offer effective non‑hormonal strategies, including lifestyle, supplements, and non‑hormonal prescriptions based on your needs.

Will menopause management affect my long‑term health?

Our plans consider bone, brain, and heart health. We coordinate labs and screenings as appropriate for your history and goals.

How often are follow‑ups?

Typically every 6–12 weeks initially, then periodically. We tailor the cadence to your response and preferences.

Is this confidential?

Absolutely. We maintain strict privacy for all consultations and treatments.
