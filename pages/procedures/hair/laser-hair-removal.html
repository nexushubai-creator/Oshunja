Overview

Our dedicated laser suite uses diode and Nd:YAG wavelengths to respect melanated skin while efficiently targeting unwanted hair. Treatment plans begin with patch testing, medication review, and a realistic timeline for each body zone.

We coordinate visits around waxing, shaving, and travel so you can maintain protective styles or active routines without irritation. Cooling technology, post-care botanicals, and sunscreen coaching protect tone and texture between sessions.

Procedure Details

  • Consultation includes Fitzpatrick skin typing, medical history review, and discreet test pulses.
  • Treatment zones are cleansed, marked, and treated with dynamic cooling to maintain comfort.
  • Settings are adjusted per area to balance efficacy and pigment safety across the body.
  • Calming gel and mineral SPF are applied before confirming aftercare and next appointments.

Anesthesia: Integrated cooling technology keeps skin comfortable; topical numbing is optional for sensitive zones.

Duration: 20 to 60 minutes depending on the number and size of treated areas.

Immediate Aftercare

  • Avoid intense heat, workouts, and direct sun for 24 hours to limit inflammation.
  • Use gentle cleansers, fragrance-free moisturiser, and SPF 50 daily between sessions.
  • Shave instead of wax or pluck for the duration of your series to protect the follicle cycle.

Possible Side Effects & Precautions

  • Mild redness or warmth similar to a sunburn that dissipates within a few hours.
  • Temporary swelling around follicles (perifollicular edema) indicating effective treatment.
  • Rare pigment shift when sun exposure or aftercare is not followed; typically self-resolving.

Benefits & Results

  • Long-term hair reduction with softer regrowth and fewer ingrown hairs.
  • Melanin-aware protocols minimise risk of burns or post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation.
  • Centralized concierge scheduling keeps face, bikini, and body timelines on track.

Timeline: Plan 6 to 8 sessions spaced four to six weeks apart, with maintenance touch-ups every 6 to 12 months.

Candidates & Considerations

Ideal Candidates

  • Individuals seeking low-maintenance smoothness and fewer ingrown hairs.
  • Clients able to commit to consistent appointments and sun-protection routines.
  • Women and men wanting to treat multiple zones—from facial hair to full body—in one location.

Important Considerations

  • Recent tanning or self-tanner use requires rescheduling until melanin levels stabilise.
  • Photosensitising medications or acne treatments may need adjustment before treatment.
  • Hormonal conditions such as PCOS can influence maintenance frequency; disclose diagnoses for tailored planning.

Complementary Treatments

  • Wellness CoachingSupport hydration and lifestyle habits that keep skin calm between sessions.
  • Sauna RitualsSchedule sauna visits 48 hours after laser to relax muscles without over-heating skin.

Caribbean Care Notes

  • Book morning appointments and wear breathable fabrics to avoid trapping heat against treated areas.
  • Reapply mineral sunscreen every two hours outdoors to protect freshly treated skin.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is laser hair removal safe for dark skin?

Yes. We utilise Nd:YAG wavelengths and melanin-aware settings specifically designed for deeper skin tones.

How should I prepare for my appointment?

Shave the area 24 hours prior, skip lotions or deodorant the day of, and avoid sun exposure for two weeks.

Can I treat multiple areas in one visit?

Absolutely. We can bundle face, bikini, underarms, and body zones within a single concierge appointment.
