Overview

Laser hair removal at OSHUNJA uses advanced technology safe for all skin tones, including darker Caribbean complexions. Our medical-grade lasers target hair follicles to provide permanent hair reduction on face, underarms, bikini, legs, and body‚Äîfreeing you from constant shaving, waxing, and ingrown hairs.

Kingston's warm climate makes unwanted body hair particularly frustrating. Our laser technology is calibrated specifically for melanin-rich skin, ensuring safe and effective treatments without the risk of hyperpigmentation or scarring common with older laser systems.

Most clients need 6-8 sessions spaced several weeks apart for optimal results. Once complete, you'll enjoy smooth, hair-free skin indefinitely with only occasional maintenance treatments as needed.

How It Works & What To Expect

1

Consultation & Skin Assessment

We evaluate your skin tone, hair color, and treatment areas. Our lasers are safe for all skin types, including darker Caribbean complexions. We develop a customized treatment schedule based on your hair growth patterns.

2

Pre-Treatment Preparation

You'll shave the treatment area (do not wax or pluck!). We apply cooling gel and provide protective eyewear. The area is cleansed and prepared for laser treatment.

3

Laser Treatment

The laser targets hair follicles with precise pulses of light energy, destroying the follicle without damaging surrounding skin. You'll feel quick snapping sensations. Treatment duration varies by area‚Äî15 minutes for underarms, up to 60 minutes for full legs.

4

Post-Treatment Care

Cooling gel is applied to soothe skin. You'll receive aftercare instructions and schedule your next session (typically 4-8 weeks apart depending on treatment area and hair growth cycle).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is laser hair removal safe for dark skin?

Yes! Our advanced laser technology is specifically calibrated for melanin-rich Caribbean skin. Unlike older lasers, our system safely targets hair follicles without damaging surrounding darker skin, minimizing risk of burns or hyperpigmentation.

How many sessions will I need?

Most patients need 6-8 sessions for up to 90% permanent hair reduction. Hair grows in cycles, and laser only affects actively growing hair. Sessions are spaced 4-8 weeks apart depending on treatment area.

Does laser hair removal hurt?

Most describe it as quick snapping sensations‚Äîtolerable for most. Our cooling technology minimizes discomfort significantly. Sensitive areas (bikini, underarms) feel more intense but brief. No anesthesia needed.

Is it really permanent?

Yes‚Äîlaser destroys hair follicles permanently. After 6-8 sessions, most see 80-90% permanent reduction. Hormonal changes or new follicle growth may require occasional maintenance (every 1-2 years), but dramatic reduction is lasting.

Can I shave between sessions?

Yes, you must shave! Shaving is required before each session and allowed between treatments. Never wax, pluck, or thread‚Äîthese remove the hair follicle that laser needs to target.

What about blonde, gray, or red hair?

Laser targets pigment in hair. Dark hair responds best. Blonde, red, light gray, or white hair has minimal pigment and won't respond well to laser treatment. A consultation can determine if you're a good candidate.

Can I get laser if I'm tanned?

No‚Äîtanned skin increases burn risk significantly. Avoid sun exposure, tanning beds, and self-tanners for 4-6 weeks before treatment. This is challenging in Jamaica but essential for safety. Schedule during periods when you can avoid sun.

What areas can be treated?

Any area with unwanted hair: face, underarms, bikini (including Brazilian), legs, arms, chest, back, abdomen. We treat all body areas safely and effectively for both men and women.

How much does laser hair removal cost in Jamaica?

Cost varies by treatment area size and number of sessions. Small areas (underarms, upper lip) cost less than large areas (full legs, back). We offer package pricing for multiple sessions at reduced rates. Contact us for detailed pricing.

Will it help with ingrown hairs?

Absolutely! Laser hair removal is one of the best treatments for chronic ingrown hairs and razor bumps. By permanently reducing hair growth, you eliminate the root cause of ingrowns‚Äîrecurring hair trying to break through skin.
