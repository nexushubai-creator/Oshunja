Overview

Platelet-rich plasma hair restoration leverages your own growth factors to restart sluggish follicles, extend the hair-growth cycle, and calm inflammatory shedding. Our physicians evaluate hormones, nutrition, and styling habits before precisely mapping injection zones across the scalp.

Every series is partnered with lifestyle guidance for braids, locs, silk presses, or natural textures so regrowth integrates seamlessly. Concierge follow-up ensures your boosters, lab work, and at-home topicals stay on rhythm with Kingston’s climate and your schedule.

Procedure Details

  • Digital trichoscopy, scalp photography, and lab review establish baselines and focus zones.
  • A small blood draw is processed in-clinic to yield highly concentrated platelet-rich plasma.
  • Topical anesthetic and vibration therapy minimize discomfort before precise injections or microneedling.
  • PRP is layered across thinning regions and hair partings, then sealed with growth serums.
  • You leave with a custom regimen covering supplements, cleansing cadence, and booster schedule.

Anesthesia: Topical lidocaine and scalp vibration maintain comfort throughout the session.

Duration: Approximately 60 minutes including comfort measures and post-care review.

Immediate Aftercare

  • Avoid washing or styling the scalp for 24 hours so growth factors absorb fully.
  • Sleep on a clean pillowcase and skip hats or tight headwear for the first night.
  • Pause anti-inflammatory medications for two days to support the healing cascade.
  • Resume gentle cleansing with sulfate-free shampoo on day two and apply prescribed serums.

Possible Side Effects & Precautions

  • Mild tenderness or fullness at injection points typically settles within 24 hours.
  • Transient redness or pinpoint bleeding can appear immediately post-service.
  • Minor swelling along the hairline may persist for up to 48 hours before subsiding.

Benefits & Results

  • Encourages thicker strands and reduced shedding in traction, hormonal, or stress-related loss.
  • Improves scalp health and circulation without altering curl pattern or protective styles.
  • Pairs with topical or systemic therapies for a layered regrowth strategy.

Timeline: Expect reduced shedding within four weeks, visible volume shifts by three months, and peak density around six months with boosters.

Candidates & Considerations

Ideal Candidates

  • Women and men experiencing early to moderate thinning who prefer regenerative approaches.
  • Postpartum or stress-related shedding where follicles remain viable.
  • Clients balancing protective styling, locs, or chemical services who want fuller coverage.

Important Considerations

  • Not recommended for scarring alopecia or active scalp infections until cleared.
  • Anticoagulant use may increase bruising; disclose all medications during consult.
  • Smokers or those with uncontrolled endocrine disorders may see slower results.

Complementary Treatments

  • IV TherapyVitamin-rich drips support cellular recovery and hair growth between sessions.
  • Metabolic Health TestingIdentify hormonal or nutritional drivers that influence hair density and shedding.

Caribbean Care Notes

  • Schedule morning appointments to avoid midday heat directly after treatment.
  • Protect the scalp with breathable wraps or hats once the 24-hour healing window has passed.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many PRP sessions will I need?

Most guests complete an initial series of three treatments spaced four weeks apart, followed by boosters every six to twelve months.

When will I see new growth?

Expect decreased shedding after the first session, with visible density improving across months three to six.

Can I combine PRP with relaxers or colour?

Yes—schedule chemical services at least one week after PRP and follow our stylist-approved aftercare for optimum results.
